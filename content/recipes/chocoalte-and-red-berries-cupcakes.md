## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup of maple syrup
- 1/4 cup of milk
- 1/3 cup of extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tsps of vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups of almond meal
- 1 tsps of baking powder
- 1/3 cup of cocoa powder
- frozen or fresh red fruits

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C
2. Line a muffin tin with paper cases
3. Place vanilla , maple syrup , milk , oil and eggs in a jug and whisk to combine
4. Place the almond meal , baking powder and cocoa in a bowl
5. Add the maple mixture to the dry ingredients and mix to combine
6. Half fill each paper case with mixture , sprinkle with red fruits and top with remaining mixture
7. Bake for 25 minutes or until firm
8. Allow to cool before lifting
