## Ingredients

- 1kg of potatoes
- 1/2 cup of walnuts roughly chopped
- 1/2 cup of fine cracked wheat (bulgur) washed and drained
- A bunch of chopped mint leaves 
- 1 medium onion
- Sea salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Boil potatoes in a pot of salted water until tender but still firm,drain,peal and mash with potato masher
2. Chop the onion
3. Chop the mint leaves
4. Mix potatoes, cracked wheat, mint, onion, walnuts, salt and pepper with your hands until well combined and creamy
5. Decorate with mint leaves and drizzle with olive oil
