## Ingredients

### For the Dough

- 1 cup of milk
- 50g unsalted butter
- 525g plain flour
- 110g of brown sugar 
- 1 egg
- 2 1/4 tsp dried yeast

### For the Bun

- 120g of walnuts, chopped
- 90g brown sugar
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 50g butter
- 1 lightly beaten egg
- 2 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Place milk and butter in a small pan over low heat until butter is melted
2. Place the flour,sugar,egg,yeast and milk mixture in a bowl of an electric mixer and mix on low speed until the dough comes together
3. Knead dough on a floured surface place in a bowl cover and set aside for 1 1/2 hours or until doubled in size
4. Mix the walnuts,sugar and cinnamon in a bowl
5. Roll out the dough into a ~50x30cm rectangle
6. Brush it with butter
7. Sprinkle it with the walnut mixture
8. Cut it into ~5x55cm strips
9. Roll the strips up tightly
10. Place one strip after another in a lightly greased springform caked tin lined with baking paper, creating one large spiral
11. Cover and set aside in a warm place until risen
12. Brush the bun with a beaten egg
13. Bake for 40-45min or until golden brown
14. Brush with the maple syrup
