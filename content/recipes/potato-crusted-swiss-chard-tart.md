## Ingredients

### For the Potato Base

- 6–7 medium-small potatoes, boiled with skins on
- Salt, to taste
- Crushed black pepper, to taste

### For the Filling

- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- 500g Swiss chard, steamed and well squeezed
- 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- Sea salt, to taste
- Crushed black pepper, to taste
- 500ml cooking cream
- 3 eggs
- 250g mozzarella cheese, shredded

## Instructions

### Potato Base

1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
2. Boil potatoes with skins until tender; drain.
3. Mash potatoes directly in a tart pan; season with salt and pepper.
4. Press mashed potatoes evenly over the base and up the sides to form a crust.

### Filling & Bake

1. In a pan, sauté the onion in olive oil until soft and golden.
2. Add crushed garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes.
3. Stir in the steamed Swiss chard; season with salt and pepper and cook briefly. Remove from heat.
4. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the cooking cream, then add the chard mixture and mix well.
5. Pour the filling into the potato crust and spread evenly.
6. Sprinkle mozzarella evenly on top.
7. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until the top is golden and the filling is set.
8. Rest a few minutes before slicing and serving.
