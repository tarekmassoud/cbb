## Ingredients

- A batch of fresh green olives
- 1 tbsp coarse salt
- 2 lemon wedges
- 1 chili pepper (optional)
- A drop of olive oil

## Instructions

1. Combine olives, coarse salt, lemon wedges, chili pepper if using, and a drop of olive oil in a bowl or jar.
2. Mix everything well.
3. Stir every day to keep the top from drying out.
4. Ready in about five days. Serve with labneh or as part of a mezze.
