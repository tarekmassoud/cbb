## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
- 4 small red onions, cut into quarters
- 15 mushrooms, halved or quartered
- 2 red bell peppers, chopped
- 2 yellow bell peppers, chopped

### Seasoning

- 1 tbsp oregano
- 1 tsp salt
- Black pepper, to taste

### Marinade

- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tsp garlic powder

### To Serve

- Cooked quinoa
- Fresh avocado, sliced

## Instructions

1. Place the cubed chicken in a large bowl. Add oregano, salt, and black pepper, and mix well.
2. Add the onions, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
3. Pour in the soy sauce, sesame oil, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder.
4. Mix everything together so the marinade coats all the ingredients evenly.
5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
6. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray.
7. Cook a portion of the chicken and vegetables in the basket at 180°C for about 7 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly golden.
8. Serve warm with cooked quinoa and fresh avocado slices.
