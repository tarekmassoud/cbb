## Ingredients
### Cake
- 3 eggs
- 165g brown sugar  
- 150g vanilla extract  
- 155g self-rising flour  
- 120g almond meal  
- 1 tsp lemon rind  
- 2 tbsp amaretto  
- 75g melted butter  
- 40g flaked almonds  

### Syrup
- 250ml water  
- 220g brown sugar  
- 1 vanilla bean  
- 60ml amaretto  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a 24cm tin.  
2. Beat eggs, sugar, and vanilla until tripled in volume.  
3. Add flour, almond meal, rind, butter, and amaretto; mix gently.  
4. Bake 35 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean.  
5. Simmer syrup ingredients 10 minutes, then drizzle over cooled cake.  
6. Sprinkle with almonds and serve.
