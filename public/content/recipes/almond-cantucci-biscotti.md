## Ingredients
- 2 eggs  
- 250g white flour  
- 180g brown sugar, plus extra for rolling  
- Pinch of salt  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 25g butter  
- 175g almonds

## Instructions
1. Mix eggs, flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and butter to a dough; fold in almonds.  
2. Chill 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 190°C.  
3. Divide dough in two; shape each into a 3 cm thick, 30 cm long log. Roll lightly in sugar and place on a tray.  
4. Bake 25 minutes (middle shelf). Cool 10 minutes.  
5. Slice diagonally into 1 cm pieces. Return cut-side down and bake 10 minutes until crisp.
