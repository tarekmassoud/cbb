## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup brown sugar  
- 225g butter, softened  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- 1/8 tsp salt  
- 1 cup almond meal  
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour  
- Icing sugar, for dusting  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  
2. Cream sugar and butter.  
3. Add vanilla and cinnamon.  
4. Mix in almond meal and flour.  
5. On a floured surface, shape crescents.  
6. Place on tray and bake 15–20 minutes.  
7. Dust with icing sugar.
