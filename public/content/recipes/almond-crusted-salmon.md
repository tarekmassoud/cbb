## Ingredients
- 4 salmon fillets (about 300 g each)
- 3/4 cup almond flour
- 3 tbsp coconut sugar (or brown sugar)
- 1 tsp paprika (or smoked paprika)
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 4 heaped tsp Dijon mustard

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
- Pat salmon dry and spread about 1 tbsp Dijon on each fillet.
- In a bowl, mix almond flour, coconut sugar, paprika, cayenne, and salt.
- Coat fillets completely with the almond mixture and set on a lined tray.
- Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden and cooked through.
