## Ingredients
- 750ml water  
- 200g amar el din or candied apricot  
- 150g sugar  
- Pinch of salt  
- 20ml orange blossom water  
- 30ml rose water  
- 300g powdered milk  
- 50g pine nuts  

## Instructions
1. Cut apricots into small pieces.  
2. Combine with water, sugar, and salt in a saucepan; simmer until dissolved.  
3. Add milk and boil briefly.  
4. Remove from heat, stir in rose and blossom water.  
5. Chill mixture, then churn in an ice cream maker.  
6. Freeze until set and top with pine nuts.
