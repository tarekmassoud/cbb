## Ingredients
- 2 cups (180g) rolled oats  
- 1 cup (150g) plain flour  
- ½ cup (110g) caster sugar  
- ½ cup (60g) desiccated coconut  
- ½ cup (115g) golden syrup  
- 125g unsalted butter, chopped  
- 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda  
- 2 tbsp hot water

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line baking trays.  
2. In a large bowl, combine oats, flour, sugar, and coconut.  
3. In a small saucepan over low heat, stir together golden syrup and butter until melted.  
4. In a small bowl, mix bicarbonate of soda with hot water. Stir into the syrup mixture.  
5. Pour the syrup into the dry ingredients and mix until combined.  
6. Spoon 7 cm rounds onto trays, leaving space to spread.  
7. Bake 10–12 minutes or until deep golden. Cool on trays.
