## Ingredients

### Dumplings
- 2 sheets ready-made shortcrust pastry  
- 3 small apples  
- 110g brown sugar  
- 1 tbsp cinnamon  
- Nutmeg (grated)  
- 20g butter (8 cubes)  
- 1 egg for glaze  

### Sauce
- 2 tbsp butter  
- 1/3 cup brown sugar  
- 1 tbsp whiskey  
- 1 tsp vanilla  

## Instructions
1. Peel + core apples.  
2. Mix sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg.  
3. Roll pastry to 12×18 inch rectangle; cut into 6 squares.  
4. Fill apples with mixture; top with butter cube.  
5. Wrap apples; seal well.  
6. Arrange in dish; brush with egg.  
7. Pour sauce over.  
8. Bake 40–45 minutes.
