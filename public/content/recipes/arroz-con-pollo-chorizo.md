## Ingredients
- 1.2 kg chicken thighs/drumsticks  
- 200 g chorizo, sliced  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 large bell peppers (red and yellow), sliced  
- 1 large onion, finely chopped  
- 1 garlic clove, finely chopped  
- 1/2 tsp paprika  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tsp black pepper  
- 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice  
- 3 cups chicken stock  
- Saffron threads – 1/2 tsp (steep in warm stock)  
- 1 tsp dried oregano  
- 1 tsp ground cumin  
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes  
- 1/2 cup white wine  
- 1 cup frozen green peas  
- 3 bay leaves  
- 1/4 cup capers  
- 1/4 cup green olives, finely chopped

## Instructions
1. Rub chicken with paprika, salt, and pepper.  
2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a deep pan/paella pan over medium-high. Brown chorizo; remove. Brown chicken; remove.  
3. Add a bit more oil, then sauté peppers and onion until soft; add garlic and sauté briefly.  
4. Stir in saffron (with its warm stock), paprika, oregano, cumin, and chili flakes.  
5. Return chicken and chorizo; add rice and stir to coat. Pour in remaining stock and white wine; add bay leaves. Bring to a boil.  
6. Reduce to low, cover, and simmer 20–25 minutes until rice is nearly tender.  
7. Add peas, capers, and olives; cook another 10 minutes on low. Rest 5 minutes and serve.
