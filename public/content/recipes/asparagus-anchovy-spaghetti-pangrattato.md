## Ingredients
### Pangrattato
- 2 slices stale sourdough bread, torn into pieces
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp fennel seeds
- Pinch of salt

### Pasta
- 200g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 large cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 chilli, finely chopped
- 4 anchovy fillets
- 200g asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-size pieces
- ½ cup pasta cooking water, as needed
- ½ cup parmesan, finely grated
- 1 lemon, zest
- Black pepper

## Instructions
- For the pangrattato, pulse bread and fennel seeds to coarse crumbs. Toast in 2 tbsp olive oil over medium heat until golden; season with a pinch of salt.
- Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some cooking water.
- In a large pan, warm olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic, chilli, and anchovies; cook 1–2 minutes. Add asparagus and fry 3–4 minutes.
- Toss in the pasta with a splash of cooking water, parmesan, lemon zest, and black pepper. Adjust seasoning.
- Serve topped with pangrattato.
