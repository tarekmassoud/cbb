## Ingredients
- 6 Chinese cabbage leaves  
- 6 baby spinach leaves  
- 4 Lebanese cucumbers, diced small  
- 2 medium avocados, diced small  
- 1 tsp soy sauce  
- 1 tsp rice vinegar  
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil  
- 1 tsp black sesame seeds  
- Pinch of salt  

## Instructions
1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, toasted sesame oil, and a pinch of salt.  
2. In a large bowl, gently toss diced cucumbers and avocados with the dressing to coat evenly.  
3. Place a Chinese cabbage leaf on a serving plate, top with a baby spinach leaf, and fill with the avocado–cucumber mixture.  
4. Sprinkle black sesame seeds over the tops. Arrange on a platter and serve immediately.
