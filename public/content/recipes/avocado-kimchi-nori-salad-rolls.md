## Ingredients

### Rolls
- 2 ripe avocados  
- Juice of 2 limes  
- 8 sheets nori  
- 1 1/2 cups chopped lettuce  
- 3 medium carrots, julienned  
- 1 cup kimchi, chopped  
- 8 mint sprigs  
- 12 cilantro sprigs

### Coconutâ€“Amino Sauce
- 1/4 cup coconut aminos  
- Juice of 1 lime  
- 1/2 tsp fresh ginger, grated  
- 1 tbsp sesame oil  
- 1/2 tsp sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Mash avocados with lime juice.  
2. Lay nori sheets on a board; spread a spoonful of avocado, then add lettuce, carrots, kimchi, and herbs.  
3. Moisten the top edge with water and roll tightly. Repeat.  
4. Mix sauce ingredients and serve rolls with the dipping sauce.
