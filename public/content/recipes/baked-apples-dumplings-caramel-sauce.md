## Ingredients

### Caramel Base
- 1 1/2 cups sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 150g butter  
- 125ml milk  
- 3–4 red apples  

### Dumplings
- 2 cups self-raising flour  
- 55g brown sugar  
- 150g cold butter  
- 1 egg  
- 1/2 cup milk  
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar  
- 1 1/2 cups water  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  
2. Make caramel: heat sugar, butter, vanilla, and water until dissolved.  
3. Bring to boil, then pour into baking dish.  
4. Combine flour, sugar, and butter; rub to fine crumbs.  
5. Add egg + milk; form dough.  
6. Form dumplings and place among apples in caramel.  
7. Bake 30 minutes or until golden.
