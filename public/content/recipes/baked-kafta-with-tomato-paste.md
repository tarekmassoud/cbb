## Ingredients

### For the Kafta

- 700 g ground lamb or beef meat
- 1 medium onion, finely processed with its juice
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp 7 spices
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)

### For the Baking Base

- 4 large onions, cut into wedges
- 4 medium tomatoes, peeled and finely chopped
- 1 1/2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup water mixed with the extra 1/2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- A handful of cherry tomatoes
- 2 green chili peppers, whole or halved

### For Serving

- Steamed rice
- Toasted pine nuts

## Instructions

### Prepare the Kafta

1. Mix the ground meat with the processed onion and its juice.
2. Add salt, black pepper, 7 spices, and cinnamon.
3. Knead well for 2 to 3 minutes until cohesive.
4. Shape into finger-shaped kebabs, about 5 cm long.

### Prepare the Base

1. In a baking dish, combine the onion wedges and chopped tomatoes.
2. Add the salt, black pepper, cumin, olive oil, and 1 tbsp tomato paste, mix well, then add the 1/2 cup water mixed with 1/2 tbsp tomato paste.

### Bake

1. Bake at 180°C for 20 to 25 minutes until the onions start to soften.
2. Sear the kebab fingers briefly in a little olive oil until lightly browned on the outside.
3. Remove the tray from the oven.
4. Place the browned kebab fingers over the baked vegetable base.
5. Add the cherry tomatoes and the 2 chili peppers between the kebabs.
6. Return to the oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes more until fully cooked and juicy.

### Serve

1. Serve hot with steamed rice and toasted pine nuts.
