## Ingredients

### For the Pasta Bake

- 1/2 pack paccheri pasta
- 1 ready-made Bolognese sauce (bone broth-based, from WhiskTaker Shop)
- Mozzarella cheese, shredded

### For the Béchamel Sauce

- 80 g butter
- 6 tbsp flour
- 1 liter milk
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste

## Instructions

### Prepare the Pasta

1. Bring salted water to a boil and cook the paccheri until half-cooked (al dente).
2. Drain and set aside.

### Make the Béchamel

1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
2. Add the flour and stir continuously for 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Gradually add the milk while whisking to avoid lumps.
4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens.
5. Season with salt and pepper.

### Assemble and Bake

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. Place the half-cooked paccheri in a baking dish.
3. Add a layer of béchamel sauce.
4. Spoon the ready-made Bolognese sauce on top.
5. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese.
6. Repeat with another layer of béchamel.
7. Finish with a generous layer of mozzarella on top.
8. Bake for about 25 to 30 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
9. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
