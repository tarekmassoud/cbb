## Ingredients
- 1 block ricotta cheese  
- 1 medium onion, sliced  
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes  
- 1/2 cup black olives  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt and pepper  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C.  
2. Arrange tomatoes, onion, and olives in a baking dish.  
3. Place ricotta in the center, drizzle with olive oil, and season.  
4. Bake for 20 minutes until vegetables are tender and ricotta slightly golden.  
5. Serve with crackers or toasted bread.
