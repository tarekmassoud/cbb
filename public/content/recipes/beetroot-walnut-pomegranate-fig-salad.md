## Ingredients
- 1½ cups (150g) walnuts  
- 2 tbsp maple syrup  
- 8 baby beetroots (400g), trimmed and thinly sliced  
- 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil  
- 1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar  
- Sea salt and cracked black pepper, to taste  
- Seeds from 1 pomegranate  
- Fresh figs, sliced  
- 4 cups baby red-vein sorrel leaves  

### Dressing
- 150g soft goat’s cheese  
- 1/2 cup (125ml) milk  
- 1 tsp white balsamic vinegar  

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 180°C. In a small bowl, toss walnuts with maple syrup; spread on a parchment-lined tray and bake 8–10 minutes until golden. Cool.  
2. In a large bowl, toss sliced beetroot with olive oil, white balsamic, salt, and pepper.  
3. Add sorrel, figs, caramelized walnuts, and pomegranate seeds to a platter.  
4. Blend goat’s cheese, milk, and white balsamic until smooth; drizzle over salad and serve.
