## Ingredients
### For the dough
- 4.5 cups plain flour  
- 1 cup warm water  
- 2¼ tsp dry yeast  
- 1 tbsp kosher salt  
- 2 large eggs at room temperature  
- ½ cup vegetable oil  
- ¼ cup honey  
- 1 egg beaten + 1 tsp water for brushing  

### For the filling
- 1 jar Belgo hazelnut chocolate spread  

## Instructions
1. Combine 1 cup flour with yeast and warm water. Let sit 1 hour.  
2. Add oil, honey, eggs, and mix by hand. Add remaining flour and salt.  
3. Knead until smooth. Let rise 1–3 hours.  
4. Divide dough in half, roll each into rectangles.  
5. Spread with Belgo chocolate, roll, and twist into rings.  
6. Place in a springform tin, cover, and let rest 35–45 minutes.  
7. Brush with egg wash, sprinkle with brown sugar, and bake at 180°C for 35 minutes until golden.
