## Ingredients
- 250 g butter  
- 200 g white chocolate  
- 495 g sugar  
- 275 ml water  
- 400 g plain flour  
- 100 g self-raising flour  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 230 g raspberries  
- 375 ml whipping cream  

### For the custard
- 280 ml milk, 280 ml cream  
- 45 g butter  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 110 g sugar  
- 1/4 cup cornflour  
- 80 ml water  
- 5 egg yolks

## Instructions
1. Melt butter, chocolate, sugar, and water over low heat; cool slightly.  
2. Whisk in flours and vanilla; bake in a lined 24 cm tin at 120°C for ~2 hours. Cool.  
3. Cook custard to thicken; cool. Whip cream.  
4. Split cake, layer with custard and berries; chill 2–3 hours before slicing.
