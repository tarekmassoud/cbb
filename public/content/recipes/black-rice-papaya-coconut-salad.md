## Ingredients
- ½ cup black rice, cooked and cooled
- 200g snow peas, blanched
- 2 cups shredded red cabbage
- 1 small papaya, thinly sliced
- 1 small chilli, thinly sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- Fresh coconut, shaved or sliced

### Coconut Dressing
- ½ cup coconut milk
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions
- Cook black rice and cool. Blanch snow peas briefly, then chill.
- On a large platter, layer rice, cabbage, papaya, snow peas, chilli, basil, and coconut.
- Whisk dressing ingredients until smooth. Drizzle over the salad and serve.
