## Ingredients
- 4–5 firm white fish fillets  
- Salt & black pepper  
- 3 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 cup diced onion  
- 3 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 tsp paprika  
- 1 tsp ground cumin  
- ¼ tsp red pepper flakes  
- 1 cup coconut milk  
- 1 tbsp soy sauce  
- 1 yellow and ½ red bell pepper, sliced  
- 1 bunch cilantro, chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a pot, add onion, garlic, and spices.  
2. Stir in coconut milk, soy sauce, and peppers. Simmer 3 minutes.  
3. Season fish, add to pot, and cook 20 minutes until fish is tender.  
4. Serve with rice and cilantro.
