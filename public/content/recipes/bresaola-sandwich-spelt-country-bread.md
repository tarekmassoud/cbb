## Ingredients

- 2 slices spelt country bread  
- A few slices Parmesan cheese  
- 2–3 fresh mozzarella slices  
- 2–3 tomato slices  
- 4–5 thin slices bresaola  
- A small bunch fresh rocca (arugula)  
- Extra virgin olive oil  
- Salt and black pepper, to taste  

## Instructions

1. Lightly brush both sides of the bread slices with olive oil.  
2. Toast the bread in a pan or toaster until golden and crisp.  
3. Layer Parmesan slices over one slice of toasted bread.  
4. Add the mozzarella slices.  
5. Top with tomato slices and season lightly with salt and pepper.  
6. Add the bresaola, then a generous handful of fresh rocca.  
7. Drizzle a little extra olive oil if desired.  
8. Close with the second slice of bread and press gently.  
9. Toast again briefly to warm the sandwich, then slice in half and serve immediately.
