## Ingredients

### For the dough
- 500 g broccoli florets, roughly chopped  
- 1/3 cup almond meal  
- 2/3 cup finely grated Parmesan  
- 1 egg  
- Sea salt and cracked black pepper

### For the tart
- 800 g pumpkin, peeled and chopped  
- 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil  
- Sea salt and pepper  
- 200 g fresh ricotta  
- 8–10 sage leaves  
- 150 g soft goat cheese, broken into large pieces  
- 3 eggs

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a tart tin with baking paper.  
2. Pulse broccoli in a processor until finely chopped. Mix with almond meal, Parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper. Press dough into base and sides of the tin.  
3. Place pumpkin on a tray; drizzle with oil; season. Roast 20 minutes.  
4. Whisk together eggs, ricotta, salt, and pepper. Arrange roasted pumpkin in the crust; pour egg mixture over; top with goat’s cheese and sage.  
5. Bake 25–30 minutes until golden and set. Rest before slicing.
