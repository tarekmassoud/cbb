## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 leek, thinly sliced  
- 1 garlic clove, minced  
- 400 g broccoli, florets and stems chopped (reserve a few florets)  
- 4 cups water or light stock  
- 2 cups baby spinach  
- 1 tsp sea salt, plus more to taste  
- 200 ml coconut milk (optional for creaminess)  
- 2 tbsp toasted hazelnuts, chopped  
- 1 tsp chia seeds  
- Black pepper

## Instructions
1. Air-fry or pan-char a few broccoli florets until lightly browned; set aside for topping.  
2. In a pot, warm oil. Sauté leek and garlic 3–4 minutes until soft.  
3. Add broccoli, water or stock, and salt. Simmer 10 minutes until tender.  
4. Stir in spinach and coconut milk; cook 2 minutes.  
5. Blend smooth with an immersion blender. Adjust seasoning.  
6. Serve topped with charred broccoli, hazelnuts, chia, and pepper.
