## Ingredients
- 3 thick steaks
- Salt
- Oil
- Butter
- Garlic
- Thyme

## Instructions
1. Pat steak dry and season.
2. Sear 3 minutes per side.
3. Add butter, garlic, thyme and baste.
4. Rest before slicing.
