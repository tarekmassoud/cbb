## Ingredients
- 2 onions
- 2 garlic cloves
- Ginger
- 500 g butternut squash
- 500 g sweet potatoes
- 1 litre vegetable stock
- Cumin & turmeric

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger.
2. Add spices.
3. Add vegetables and stock.
4. Simmer 20 minutes.
5. Blend until smooth.
6. Serve with croutons.
