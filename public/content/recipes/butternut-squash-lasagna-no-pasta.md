## Ingredients

### Meat Sauce
- 2 tsp olive oil  
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped  
- 1 large onion, chopped  
- 500g minced beef  
- 400g tomato sauce  
- 1 tbsp tomato purée  
- 120ml water  
- Sea salt & cracked black pepper  

### Béchamel Sauce
- 120g butter  
- 6 tbsp all-purpose flour  
- 6 cups milk  
- Salt and pepper  

### Lasagna Layers
- 1 large butternut squash, peeled and thinly sliced  
- 340g grated mozzarella  
- 2 fresh mozzarella balls, sliced  
- Fresh basil leaves  

## Instructions

### Meat Sauce
1. Heat olive oil in a deep pan over high heat.  
2. Add garlic and onion; cook 3 minutes until soft.  
3. Add minced beef and cook until browned.  
4. Stir in tomato purée, tomato sauce, and water.  
5. Simmer 10–15 minutes until thickened. Season and set aside.

### Béchamel
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.  
2. Add flour and whisk into a smooth paste.  
3. Gradually add milk, whisking constantly.  
4. Cook until thickened. Season and let cool slightly.

### Assemble
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a 20 cm ovenproof dish.  
2. Layer butternut squash, béchamel, then meat sauce.  
3. Repeat layers until ingredients are used.  
4. Add mozzarella and basil as the final layer.  
5. Finish with béchamel and grated mozzarella.

### Bake
- Bake 45 minutes, covered for the first 30 minutes, then uncovered until golden.  
- Rest 10 minutes before serving.
