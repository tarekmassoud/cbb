## Ingredients
- 150 g brown butter  
- 30 ml milk  
- 130 g brown sugar  
- 60 g white sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 egg  
- 240 g all-purpose flour  
- 4 g baking soda (about 1 tsp)  
- 1/4 tsp salt  
- 1 bar caramel sea salt chocolate, chopped (or caramel sea salt pieces)

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 180°C and line a baking sheet with parchment.  
2. Melt brown butter over medium heat; stir until it turns golden brown. Cool slightly.  
3. In a bowl, combine cooled brown butter, milk, brown sugar, white sugar, and vanilla; mix. Beat in egg.  
4. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt; fold into wet mixture.  
5. Gently fold in chocolate pieces.  
6. Scoop 2 tbsp portions, place on tray with space between.  
7. Bake 10–12 minutes until edges are golden and centers soft. Cool briefly on tray, then move to a rack.
