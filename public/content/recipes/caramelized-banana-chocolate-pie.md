## Ingredients
- 400 g peeled bananas, sliced  
- 1/4 cup brown sugar  
- 70 g unsalted butter  
- 2 tbsp whiskey  
- 1/2 cup milk  
- 1/3 cup desiccated coconut  
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract  
- 2 eggs  
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour  
- 1/4 cup caster sugar

### Ganache
- 1/4 cup cream  
- 2 tbsp honey  
- 80 g dark chocolate  
- Sea salt flakes

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 160ºC.  
2. Caramelize bananas with sugar and 20 g butter for 4–5 min; add whiskey.  
3. Blend remaining ingredients; pour into pie dish.  
4. Bake 30 min until set; cool completely.  
5. Heat cream, honey, and chocolate; mix smooth and spread over pie.  
6. Sprinkle sea salt before serving.
