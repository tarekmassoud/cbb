## Ingredients
- 50g butter  
- 1/3 cup brown sugar  
- 8 ripe bananas, sliced  
- 4 eggs  
- 1 cup caster sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 cup almond meal  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- 1 cup flour  
- 150g butter, melted  

## Instructions
1. Melt butter and sugar to form caramel; pour into greased pan.  
2. Arrange bananas over caramel.  
3. Beat eggs, sugar, and vanilla until fluffy.  
4. Add almond meal, baking powder, flour, and melted butter; mix.  
5. Pour over bananas and bake 1 hour at 160°C.  
6. Cool 5 min, then invert onto a plate.
