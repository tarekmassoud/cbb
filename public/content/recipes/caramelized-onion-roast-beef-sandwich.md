## Ingredients
- 1/2 beef fillet (thinly sliced)  
- 1/2 cup caramelized onions  
- 2 tbsp pesto  
- 4 small baguettes  
- 3 cups chestnut mushrooms, sliced  
- 3 sprigs rosemary  
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

## Instructions
1. Toast bread in a pan with oil until crisp; remove.  
2. Sauté mushrooms with rosemary; season.  
3. Sear beef slices briefly; season.  
4. Spread pesto on bread, layer onions, beef, mushrooms, and tomato if using. Serve hot.
