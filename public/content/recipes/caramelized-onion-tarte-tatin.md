## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup sugar  
- 4 tbsp butter  
- 2 tbsp water  
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar  
- 10–12 medium onions, halved horizontally  
- 1 sheet puff pastry  
- 1 egg yolk, beaten  
- Rosemary sprigs, optional  
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C. In an oven-safe skillet, melt sugar, butter, water, and vinegar to a deep caramel.  
2. Tightly layer onion halves cut-side up into the caramel; cook a few minutes. Season.  
3. Lay puff pastry over, tucking edges. Brush with egg.  
4. Bake 25–30 minutes until pastry is puffed and golden. Rest 5 minutes, then invert onto a plate. Garnish and serve warm.
