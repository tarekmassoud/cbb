## Ingredients
- 1 3/4 cups double cream
- 2 1/4 cups sugar
- 100 g unsalted butter, chopped
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup golden syrup or maple syrup
- 8 cardamom pods, crushed
- 1/2 cup pistachios, chopped

## Instructions
- Line a 20 cm square tin. Combine cream, sugar, butter, vanilla, and syrup; heat to dissolve.
- Simmer without stirring until 124Â°C (firm-ball). Remove from heat; cool 5 minutes.
- Stir in crushed cardamom and pistachios. Pour into tin and set 8 hours or overnight.
- Cut into squares and wrap.
