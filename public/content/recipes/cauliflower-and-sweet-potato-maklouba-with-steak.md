## Ingredients
- 1 large cauliflower, florets
- 2 sweet potatoes, sliced
- 6–8 steak fillets
- Cherry tomatoes
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper
- 4 cups stock

## Instructions
1. Roast cauliflower and sweet potatoes at 200°C until golden.
2. Sear steak and tomatoes.
3. Layer steak, sweet potatoes, half rice, cauliflower, remaining rice.
4. Pour stock to cover.
5. Cover and cook 30 minutes until rice is done.
6. Rest and flip onto serving platter.
