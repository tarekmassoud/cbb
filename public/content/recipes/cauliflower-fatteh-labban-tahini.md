## Ingredients
**From LivGood**
- Tahini, Labban, Organic Chickpeas, Butter, Pine nuts

**From my garden**
- 3 organic cauliflowers  
- 1 pomegranate

**Sauce**
- 2 1/2 cups labban  
- 3 tbsp tahini  
- 4 crushed garlic cloves  
- Pinch of salt

**To finish**
- 2 tbsp butter  
- 1–2 cups grilled bread, torn  
- 1/2 cup chickpeas, drained  
- Pine nuts, toasted in butter  
- Pomegranate seeds

## Instructions
1. Cut cauliflower into florets, drizzle with olive oil, roast at 180°C for 20 minutes.  
2. Grill bread until crispy.  
3. Drain and rinse chickpeas.  
4. Mix labban, tahini, garlic, and salt.  
5. To assemble: On a platter, layer grilled bread, sauce, roasted cauliflower, chickpeas, pomegranate seeds, and butter-toasted pine nuts. Serve immediately.
