## Ingredients
- 1 small cauliflower, cut into wedges  
- 1/4 cup olive oil  
- Sea salt and black pepper  
- 1 bunch chard or kale  
- 8 eggs  
- 3 tbsp milk  
- 2 tbsp thyme leaves  
- 1 tbsp lemon zest  
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan  
- 200 g goat’s curd  
- 100 g camembert, sliced

## Instructions
1. Roast cauliflower with oil, salt, and pepper at 200ºC for 20 min.  
2. Boil chard 1 min; drain and chop.  
3. Whisk eggs with milk, herbs, zest, and cheese.  
4. Arrange chard and cauliflower in pan, pour over mixture, top with curd and camembert.  
5. Bake 30–40 min at 160ºC until golden.
