## Ingredients
- 1 cauliflower
- 250 g meat
- Garlic
- Coriander
- Olive oil
- Water

## Instructions
1. Roast cauliflower.
2. Brown meat.
3. Add garlic and coriander.
4. Add water and simmer.
5. Add cauliflower and cook 10 minutes.
6. Serve with rice.
