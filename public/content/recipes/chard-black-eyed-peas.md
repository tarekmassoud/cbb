## Ingredients

- A bunch of silverbeet or chard (stems and leaves), finely chopped
- 1 cup black-eyed peas, cooked until al dente
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- A handful of fresh coriander leaves, chopped
- Salt, to taste
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Extra virgin olive oil
- 2–3 onions, thinly sliced into wedges (for caramelizing)
- Extra olive oil for cooking

## Instructions

1. Boil the black-eyed peas until just tender; drain and set aside.
2. Blanch chopped chard in boiling water until wilted. Rinse cold and squeeze out excess water.
3. Caramelize the sliced onions in olive oil over medium heat; set aside.
4. In the same pan, sauté the diced onion and chard. Add minced garlic and coriander, then add the black-eyed peas. Season with salt and drizzle with olive oil.
5. Adjust seasoning, plate, and top with caramelized onions. Serve with lemon juice and warm saj bread.
