## Ingredients
- 2 eggs  
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil  
- 1 cup natural yogurt  
- 1 tsp lemon rind  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 1 cup desiccated coconut  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 260g flour  
- 20 cherries, pitted and halved  
- Extra sugar for sprinkling  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.  
2. Mix eggs, oil, yogurt, lemon rind, and sugar.  
3. Add coconut, flour, baking powder.  
4. Pour into pan; add cherries.  
5. Sprinkle sugar.  
6. Bake 45 minutes.
