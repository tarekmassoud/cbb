## Ingredients
- 225g unsalted butter  
- 340g half semisweet half dark chocolate  
- 415g all-purpose flour  
- 1 1/4 tsp baking powder  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- 3/4 tsp salt  
- 300g brown sugar  
- 100g granulated sugar  
- 2 eggs  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line two sheet pans with baking paper.  
2. Slowly melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat.  
3. Chop the chocolate.  
4. Whisk dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt.  
5. In another bowl, mix melted butter with sugars. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.  
6. Add dry ingredients and stir. Fold in chocolate chunks.  
7. Scoop dough onto trays.  
8. Chill dough on tray 30 minutes.  
9. Bake 10–12 minutes until golden but still soft in the center.
