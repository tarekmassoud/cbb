## Ingredients

### For the Sauce

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 3 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp yellow mustard
- 2 tbsp finely chopped cornichon
- 1 tbsp cornichon juice
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder

### For the Slaw

- A slice of cabbage, finely cut into julienne
- 2 small carrots, cut into julienne

### For the Burgers

- 4 ready-made chicken breast patties
- 4 slices cheddar cheese
- 1 large onion, cut into rounds
- 4 brioche burger buns

## Instructions

### Prepare the Slaw

1. In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, chopped cornichons, cornichon juice, Worcestershire sauce, salt, smoked paprika, black pepper, and garlic powder.
2. Add the julienned cabbage and carrots directly into the sauce and toss well until fully coated. Set aside.

### Cook the Burgers

1. Heat a pan with a drizzle of oil and cook the onion rounds until soft and caramelized. Remove and set aside.
2. In the same pan, cook the chicken patties for 5 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 5 minutes.
3. Top each patty with a slice of cheddar cheese, cover the pan, and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the cheese melts.
4. Lightly toast the brioche buns.

### Assemble

1. Add a generous amount of the creamy slaw mixture.
2. Add the cheesy chicken patty.
3. Top with the cooked onion rounds.
4. Close and serve.
