## Ingredients

- 6 to 8 chicken breasts, cut into large cubes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 2 zucchinis, sliced
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika
- 2 1/2 tsp curry powder, or to taste
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- 1 can coconut milk
- A bunch of fresh parsley
- A handful of zbib (seedless dried raisin)
- Steamed rice, for serving

## Instructions

1. In a large pan over medium heat, add 2 tbsp olive oil and the chicken cubes. Sauté for 5 minutes.
2. Season with paprika, curry powder, salt, and black pepper. Cook until lightly browned and almost cooked through.
3. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
4. In the same pan, add olive oil.
5. Sauté the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant and softened.
6. Add the bell pepper and cook for a few minutes.
7. Add the zucchini and cook until slightly tender.
8. Return the chicken to the pan with the vegetables.
9. Pour in the coconut milk and mix well.
10. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes until the sauce thickens and the chicken is fully cooked.
11. Serve alongside steamed rice, with fresh parsley and zbib on top.
