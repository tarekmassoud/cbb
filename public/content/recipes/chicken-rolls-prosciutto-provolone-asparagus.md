## Ingredients
- 4 chicken breasts  
- Kosher salt and cracked black pepper  
- 2 tbsp butter, sliced  
- 4 thin slices provolone cheese  
- 12 slices prosciutto  
- 12 asparagus spears, woody ends removed

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F). Grease a small baking dish.  
2. Pound chicken to 1.25 cm thickness; season both sides.  
3. Place butter, a slice of prosciutto, and 2 slices provolone on each. Add 3 asparagus spears.  
4. Roll up, seam down. Season and place in dish.  
5. Bake 30–35 minutes until chicken is cooked through and cheese is golden.
