## Ingredients
- 12 soft corn tortillas  
- 450g lean ground beef (or minced chicken)  
- 1 pack ready-made taco spice  
- 1 cup chicken stock  
- Shredded cheddar cheese  
- Rocca (arugula) leaves  
- Chopped tomatoes  
- Sliced red onion  
- Sliced radishes  
- Sliced jalape単os  
- Ready-made taco sauce

## Instructions
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the minced meat and break it up with a wooden spoon.
2. Cook 6 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and cooked through.
3. Reduce heat to medium. Add the taco spice and stir to coat.
4. Pour in the chicken stock and simmer 7 to 8 minutes, stirring, until slightly saucy. Remove from heat.
5. Prep the toppings: shred the cheese and slice the tomato, onion, radish, and jalape単o.
6. Warm the tortillas.
7. Fill each tortilla with taco meat and top with cheddar, rocca, tomato, onion, radish, jalape単o, and taco sauce.
