## Ingredients

### Puff Pastry
- 1 package ready-made mini puff pastry rounds  
- 1 egg, lightly whisked with 1 tsp milk  

### Filling
- 500g skinless chicken breast fillet  
- 1 bay leaf  
- 1 cinnamon stick  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 200g mushrooms, sliced  
- 3 corn cobs, cooked and kernels removed  

### Béchamel Sauce
- 75g plain flour  
- 1L milk  
- 4 tbsp butter  
- Salt and cracked black pepper  

### To Finish
- 100g grated mozzarella cheese  
- Fresh parsley leaves, to garnish  

## Instructions

### Puff Pastry
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper.  
2. Place puff pastry rounds on trays.  
3. Brush lightly with egg wash, avoiding the edges.  
4. Bake 20 minutes or until puffed and golden. Cool on a rack.

### Chicken & Vegetables
1. Place chicken in a saucepan, cover with water, add bay leaf and cinnamon stick.  
2. Simmer over medium-low heat until cooked through.  
3. Let cool slightly, then cut into small cubes.  
4. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté mushrooms for 3–4 minutes.  
5. Add corn and cook 2 minutes more. Set aside.

### Béchamel
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.  
2. Whisk in flour and cook 2 minutes.  
3. Gradually add milk, whisking constantly until thickened.  
4. Season with salt and pepper.

### Assemble
1. Stir chicken and vegetables into the béchamel.  
2. Spoon mixture into pastry cases.  
3. Top with grated mozzarella and bake 5 minutes until cheese melts.  
4. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
