## Ingredients

- 400g dark chocolate, chopped
- 125ml single cream
- 2 tbsp whisky
- Cocoa powder, for dusting

## Instructions

1. Set a heatproof bowl over a saucepan of gently simmering water
2. Add the chocolate and cream; stir until completely melted and smooth
3. Remove from the heat and stir in the whisky
4. Line a 20×30cm tin with baking paper and lightly grease
5. Pour the mixture into the tin and level the top
6. Refrigerate for about 3 hours, until firm
7. Lift out, slice into fingers, and dust with cocoa to serve
