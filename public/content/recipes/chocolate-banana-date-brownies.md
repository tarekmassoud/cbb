## Ingredients
- 10 soft dates (200 g), pitted
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 cups (180 g) almond meal
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup light olive oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a 20 cm square pan with baking paper.
2. Blitz all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
3. Spread into the pan and smooth the top.
4. Bake about 30 minutes. Cool in pan 20 minutes, then refrigerate until fully chilled.
5. Cut into squares. Store in an airtight container in the fridge.
