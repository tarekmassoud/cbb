## Ingredients

- 6 medium bananas  
- 1 jar peanut butter (to taste)  
- 300 g chocolate, melted (bain-marie)  
- 1 Tbsp coconut oil (melted with the chocolate)

## Method

1. Peel bananas, split lengthwise, and spread peanut butter inside; reassemble and insert popsicle sticks.  
2. Freeze until firm.  
3. Dip in melted chocolate mixed with coconut oil.  
4. Place on parchment and freeze again until set. Serve straight from the freezer.
