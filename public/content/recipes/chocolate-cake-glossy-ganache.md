## Ingredients

### Cake
- 250 g unsalted butter, softened  
- 230 g brown sugar  
- 3 eggs  
- 300 g all-purpose flour  
- 3 tsp baking powder  
- 1/3 cup cocoa powder  
- 1 cup Greek yogurt  
- 250 g dark chocolate, melted

### Frosting
- 180 g dark chocolate  
- 160 ml hot cream  
- 40 g unsalted butter, softened

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 160ºC. Grease a 24 cm springform and line with paper.  
2. Beat butter and sugar until light; beat in eggs one by one.  
3. Mix flour, baking powder, and cocoa; add alternately with yogurt until just combined. Fold in melted chocolate.  
4. Bake 1 hour or until a skewer tests clean; cool.  
5. For frosting, pour hot cream over chocolate, stir smooth; whisk in butter. Cool to spreading consistency and frost the cake.
