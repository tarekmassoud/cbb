## Ingredients
### Scones
- 300 g all-purpose flour  
- 20 g cocoa powder  
- 60 g brown sugar  
- 1 tbsp baking powder  
- 1/4 tsp salt  
- 80 g cold butter, cubed  
- 150 g chocolate chips  
- 1 egg  
- 3/4 cup milk  
- 1 tsp vanilla

### Vanilla glaze
- 1 cup icing sugar  
- 1 tsp milk powder (optional)  
- 2 tbsp milk

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 190°C. Line a tray.  
2. Whisk flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Rub in butter until sandy. Stir in chocolate chips.  
3. Whisk egg, milk, and vanilla; fold into dry mix until dough holds.  
4. Pat into a disc on parchment and cut into 12 wedges. Chill 15 minutes.  
5. Bake 16–18 minutes until set. Cool slightly.  
6. Mix glaze; drizzle over warm scones and serve.
