## Ingredients

### For the Dough
- 2 cups plain flour  
- 1/2 cup hazelnut meal  
- 3 tbsp brown sugar  
- 150g cold butter, chopped  
- 3–5 tbsp iced water  

### For the Pecan Filling
- 130g white sugar  
- 100g brown sugar  
- 1 cup heavy cream  
- 60g butter  
- 1 egg, whisked  
- 360g chopped toasted pecans  
- 3/4 tsp salt  

### For the Ganache
- 100g dark chocolate  
- 60g heavy cream  

## Instructions
1. Process flour, hazelnut meal, sugar, and butter until crumbs form.  
2. With the motor running, drizzle iced water until dough forms.  
3. Knead lightly, wrap in plastic, refrigerate 30 minutes.  
4. Roll out between parchment sheets and line tart tin.  
5. Preheat oven to 180°C. Blind bake 10 minutes, remove weights, bake 10 minutes more.  
6. For the caramel: heat sugars and cream until smooth; fold in butter and salt. Cool slightly.  
7. Whisk caramel into chocolate, then whisk in egg.  
8. Spread pecans over crust, pour caramel mixture on top.  
9. Bake 30 minutes. Cool completely before slicing.
