## Ingredients

### Hazelnut Pastry
- 2 cups plain flour  
- ½ cup hazelnut meal  
- 3 tbsp brown sugar  
- 150g cold butter, cubed  
- 2–3 tbsp iced water  

### Filling
- 6 egg yolks  
- 2 tbsp brown sugar  
- 2⅓ cups single cream  
- 200g dark chocolate  

## Instructions
1. Pulse pastry ingredients until crumbly, add water to form dough. Chill 30 minutes.  
2. Roll out and line tart tin. Blind bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.  
3. Heat cream until just boiling, pour over chocolate and stir smooth.  
4. Whisk yolks with sugar, combine with chocolate mixture.  
5. Pour into tart shell and bake 20 minutes until just set.
