## Ingredients
- 4–5 kg turkey or capon  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 orange, quartered  
- 4 sprigs thyme  
- 8 brown onions, halved  
- 2 garlic heads, halved  
- 1 L chicken stock  

### Thyme Brine
- 1 cup sea salt flakes  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 1 tbsp fennel seeds  
- 1 tbsp thyme leaves  
- 1 tbsp black pepper

## Instructions
1. Brine the bird 24 hours in salt, sugar, thyme, and fennel.  
2. Rinse, pat dry, and brush with oil.  
3. Stuff with orange and herbs, roast at 160°C for 2 hours 30 minutes.  
4. Glaze with brown sugar and juices; roast 20 minutes more at 200°C until golden.
