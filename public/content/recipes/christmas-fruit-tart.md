## Ingredients
### Pastry
- 400g flour  
- 300g cold butter, chopped  
- 100g brown sugar  
- 1 tsp ground ginger  
- 2 eggs  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  

### Filling
- 2 Granny Smith apples, peeled & grated  
- 80g currants  
- 120g sultanas
- 70g slivered almonds  
- 70g mixed orange and lemon peel  
- 30g brown sugar
- 30g chopped butter
- 1 beaten egg yolk
- 60ml brandy  
- 2 tbsp honey  
- icing sugar for dusting


## Instructions
1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, and ginger to form crumbs.  
2. Add eggs and vanilla; knead into dough. Chill 1 hour.  
3. Cook filling ingredients 8–10 minutes until soft.  
4. Line a tart tin with pastry, fill with fruit, and top with star-cut pastry.  
5. Bake 25–30 minutes at 180°C until golden.  
6. Cool, dust with icing sugar, and serve.
