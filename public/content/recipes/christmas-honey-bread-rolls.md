## Ingredients
- 1 cup warm milk  
- 1½ tsp yeast  
- 1 tbsp honey  
- 3½ cup bread flour  
- 2 tsp kosher salt  
- 3 tbsp butter, melted  
- 1 egg  
- 4 tsp chopped rosemary  

### Honey Butter Sauce
- 3 tbsp butter  
- ½ cup honey  

### Finishing Touches
- Fresh herbs  
- Pink peppercorns  

## Instructions
1. Combine milk, yeast, and honey; rest 5 minutes.  
2. Add flour, salt, butter, egg, and rosemary; knead until smooth.  
3. Rise 1 hour, divide into rolls, place in a dish.  
4. Brush with honey butter, bake at 176°C for 30 minutes.  
5. Decorate with herbs and serve warm.
