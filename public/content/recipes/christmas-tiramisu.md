## Ingredients
- 200 ml cream  
- 100 g dark chocolate  
- 10 sponge fingers  
- 1 cup espresso coffee  
- 50 ml sweet sherry  
- 500 g ricotta  
- 250 g mascarpone  
- 1 tsp vanilla bean paste  
- 3 tbsp honey  
- 1 mandarin, zested

## Instructions
1. Heat cream, stir in chocolate until smooth.  
2. Dip sponge fingers in coffee + sherry, arrange in dish.  
3. Blend ricotta, mascarpone, vanilla, and honey.  
4. Spread over chocolate layer, chill 2 hours, and top with zest and shaved chocolate.
