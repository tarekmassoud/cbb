## Ingredients
- 320 g all-purpose flour  
- Pinch of salt  
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon  
- 225 g unsalted butter, softened  
- 110 g icing sugar

### For dusting
- 2 tsp cinnamon  
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar

## Instructions
1. Beat butter and icing sugar until creamy.  
2. Add flour, salt, and cinnamon; mix until a soft dough forms.  
3. Wrap and chill 30 minutes. Heat oven to 180°C.  
4. Roll to 6 mm thickness. Cut shapes and place on lined trays.  
5. Bake 15 minutes until just golden at edges.  
6. Cool on a rack, then dust with cinnamon sugar.
