## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 kg salmon fillet  
- 1 tsp kosher salt  
- 1 tsp black peppercorns  
- 2 tsp coriander seeds  
- 2 tbsp fennel seeds  
- Orange wedges, to serve (optional)

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 230°C. Line a tray with parchment and place the salmon flesh-side down.  
2. Coarsely grind peppercorns, coriander, and fennel; mix with salt and rub over the salmon.  
3. Bake for 10 minutes, reduce heat to 200°C, and cook 10 minutes more or until just done.  
4. Serve on a platter with orange wedges; garnish as you like.
