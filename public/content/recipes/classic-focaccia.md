## Ingredients
- 3 cups flour  
- 1 cup warm water  
- 1 tbsp instant yeast  
- ½ cup olive oil  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tsp sugar  
- Garlic, thyme, rosemary  
- Cherry tomatoes  

## Instructions
1. Activate yeast with water and sugar.  
2. Mix with flour, salt, and olive oil.  
3. Knead 10 minutes, rest 30 minutes.  
4. Stretch into oiled pan, add toppings.  
5. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
