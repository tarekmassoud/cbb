## Ingredients
Sauce:
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1/2 kg minced beef
- 400 g tomato sauce
- 1 tbsp tomato purée
- 2 cups water
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- Salt and black pepper

Pasta and cheeses:
- 500 g lasagna sheets
- 3/4 cup grated mozzarella
- 120 g butter
- 6 tbsp flour
- 6 cups milk
- 3/4 cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Sauce: sauté garlic and onion in oil, add beef and cook. Stir in tomato sauce, purée, and water; simmer 10–15 minutes. Stir in parsley; season.
2. Béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, then milk. Simmer until thick. Stir in Parmesan; season.
3. Assemble in a 20 cm dish: béchamel, pasta, meat sauce. Repeat, finishing with béchamel and mozzarella.
4. Bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes until golden and bubbling. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.
