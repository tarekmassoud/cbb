## Ingredients
- 200g baby spinach  
- 12 baby potatoes  
- 300g green beans  
- 200g tuna  
- 2 hard boiled eggs, halved  
- 1 tbsp capers  

### Dressing
- 1/3 cup olive oil  
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar  
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard  
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley  
- Salt and pepper  

## Instructions
1. Boil potatoes and beans until tender.  
2. Arrange all ingredients on a platter.  
3. Whisk dressing and drizzle over salad.  
4. Serve chilled.
