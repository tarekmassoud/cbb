## Ingredients

- 2 packs white fish fillets (Siblou)
- Juice of 1 lemon, plus 2 tbsp extra for finishing
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 chili, finely chopped
- 2 cm piece ginger, peeled and grated
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 onion, diced
- 100 g baby carrots
- 100 g fresh tomatoes or 1/2 cup diced canned tomatoes
- 700 ml coconut cream (2 cans)
- 80 g frozen peas
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Fresh spinach (optional), for serving
- Steamed basmati rice, to serve

## Instructions

1. Cut each fish fillet into two equal pieces.
2. Place on a plate, drizzle with lemon juice, season with salt and pepper, and set aside.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-low heat.
4. Add garlic, chili, ginger, and cumin. Stir and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Add diced onion, curry powder, garam masala, and carrots.
6. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
7. Stir in fresh or canned tomatoes and cook for 1 minute.
8. Pour in coconut cream, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a low simmer.
9. Cook for 15 minutes.
10. Gently add frozen peas and the fish pieces to the curry.
11. Stir in fresh spinach, if using, and cook over low heat until the fish is cooked through, about 8 to 10 minutes.
12. Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice.
13. Serve hot with steamed basmati rice.
