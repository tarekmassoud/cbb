## Ingredients
- Base: 1 1/2 cups self-raising flour, 1/2 cup desiccated coconut, 1/2 cup sugar, 125 g melted butter, 1 egg
- Filling: 1 cup (320 g) plum or raspberry jam
- Topping: 1 cup desiccated coconut, 3/4 cup shredded coconut, 2 egg whites, 2 tbsp caster sugar

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a 20×30 cm tin.
- Mix base ingredients; press into tin. Bake 15 minutes.
- Spread jam over base.
- Whisk topping ingredients; spread evenly. Bake a further 12–15 minutes until golden.
- Cool completely before slicing.
