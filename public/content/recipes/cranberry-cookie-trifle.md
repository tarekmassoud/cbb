## Ingredients

### Cranberry Jelly
- 1/2 tbsp gelatine powder  
- 1/2 cup cranberry juice  
- 150g sugar  
- 1 cup pomegranate seeds  

### Chocolate Mousse
- 2 cups whipping cream  
- 3/4 cup dark chocolate, melted  

### Mascarpone Cream
- 1 cup mascarpone  
- 1/2 cup icing sugar  
- 1 1/2 cups single cream  
- 1 tsp vanilla  

## Instructions
1. Bloom gelatine in cranberry juice.  
2. Cook remaining juice and sugar; stir in gelatine; cool.  
3. Pour into glasses, refrigerate.  
4. Whip cream; fold in chocolate.  
5. Whisk mascarpone, icing sugar, cream, vanilla.  
6. Layer cookies, jelly, mousse, mascarpone.
