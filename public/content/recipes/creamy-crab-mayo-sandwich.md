## Ingredients

- 200 g fresh crab meat (or surimi, finely shredded)
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp finely chopped chives
- Juice of 1 yuzu (or substitute lemon or lime)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 1 sourdough baguette or rustic bread loaf
- A handful of arugula (rocket)

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the crab meat, mayonnaise, chives, yuzu (or lemon) juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well until creamy and evenly combined.  
2. Slice and toast your bread until golden and crisp.  
3. Spread a generous layer of the crab mixture over the toasted bread.  
4. Top with a handful of fresh arugula.  
5. Slice and serve immediately, ideally with a cold drink.
