## Ingredients
- 500g tagliatelle  
- 2 packs smoked salmon (200g each)  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 bunch basil leaves  
- 200g Parmesan cheese  
- 400ml cooking cream  
- 1/2 tsp black pepper  
- 1/2 tsp sea salt  
- 1 tsp pink berries  

## Instructions
1. Cut salmon into pieces; mix with basil and lemon. Refrigerate 30 minutes.  
2. Cook pasta in salted water.  
3. Heat cream; add Parmesan, salt, pepper.  
4. Add cooked pasta into the sauce.  
5. Top with marinated salmon and pink berries.
