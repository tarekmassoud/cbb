## Ingredients
- 300g carrots  
- 1 small sweet potato  
- 1 large leek  
- 500g courgettes  
- 100ml vegetable stock  
- 100ml cream  
- 2 eggs  
- Nutmeg  
- 100g grated cheese  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C.  
2. Slice vegetables thinly.  
3. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg.  
4. Whisk eggs, stock, cream.  
5. Arrange vegetables in dish; pour sauce.  
6. Sprinkle cheese; bake 30 minutes.
