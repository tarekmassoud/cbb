## Ingredients

- 150 g flour, sifted
- 250 ml milk
- 250 ml cream
- 4 eggs
- Pinch of salt
- Butter or oil, for frying

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sifted flour and a pinch of salt.
2. Make a well in the center and crack in the eggs.
3. Gradually pour in the milk and cream while whisking continuously until you have a smooth, thin batter.
4. Let the batter rest for 15 to 30 minutes to thicken slightly.
5. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil.
6. Pour a ladleful of batter into the pan, tilting and swirling to spread it into a thin, even layer.
7. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes until the edges start to lift and the bottom is golden, then flip and cook the other side for 1 minute.
8. Repeat with the remaining batter, oiling the pan as needed.
9. Serve warm with your favorite toppings or fillings.
