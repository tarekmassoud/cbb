## Ingredients
- 4 cups soaked chickpeas, drained  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 8 tbsp water (as needed)  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 2 tbsp za’atar  
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds (or pumpkin, flaxseed, black sesame)

## Instructions
1. Blend chickpeas, oil, water, baking soda, and salt to a smooth dough.  
2. Roll between sheets of baking paper to 6–4 mm thick.  
3. Peel the top paper, sprinkle with za’atar and sesame; score or cut shapes.  
4. Bake at 200°C for 15–25 minutes until dry and golden. Cool on tray.
