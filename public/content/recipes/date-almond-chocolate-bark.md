## Ingredients

- 400 g dark chocolate
- Deseeded dates
- 1/2 cup almonds

## Instructions

1. Melt the dark chocolate using a bain-marie.
2. Roast the almonds until golden and fragrant.
3. Line a tray with non-stick baking paper and arrange the deseeded dates in a single layer.
4. Sprinkle the roasted almonds over the top.
5. Pour the melted chocolate evenly over everything to cover.
6. Refrigerate for about 1 hour or until set.
7. Cut into rectangles and serve.
