## Ingredients
- 200 g fresh dates, pitted  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 tsp boiling water (as needed to blend)  
- 100 g unsalted butter, softened  
- 45 g + 30 g brown sugar (divided)  
- 1 egg, lightly beaten  
- 260 g all-purpose flour, sifted  
- 1/4 tsp baking soda  
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon  
- 3 tbsp white sugar (for dusting)

## Instructions
1. Process dates with vanilla and 45 g brown sugar to a paste.  
2. In a mixer, beat butter with remaining brown sugar 2–3 minutes; beat in egg.  
3. Mix in the date paste, then flour, baking soda, and cinnamon to form a dough.  
4. Wrap and chill 40 minutes. Heat oven to 180ºC and line trays.  
5. Roll dough 5 mm thick; cut heart shapes.  
6. Mix white sugar with a pinch of cinnamon; dust cookies.  
7. Bake 10–12 minutes until lightly golden. Cool on racks.
