## Ingredients
- 4 cups cream
- 1 vanilla bean, split and seeds scraped
- 8 egg yolks
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 200g dates, pitted and chopped
- 2 tbsp amaretto, optional
- Extra sugar for caramelising

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 150°C.
- Warm the cream with the vanilla bean and seeds; remove from heat and allow to infuse. Discard the pod.
- Whisk yolks and brown sugar until smooth. Gradually whisk in warm cream, then fold in dates and amaretto.
- Divide among 6–8 ramekins. Place in a deep baking dish and pour hot water halfway up the sides.
- Bake 25 minutes or until just set in the center. Cool, then chill 4 hours or overnight.
- Scatter each with sugar and caramelise with a torch or under a hot grill until crisp.
