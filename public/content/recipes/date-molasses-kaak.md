## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups almond flour (or finely ground almond powder)
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp ground anise
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/3 cup date molasses
- 1/3 cup tahini
- Roasted sesame seeds, for coating (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
2. In a bowl, combine almond flour, baking soda, ground anise, and cinnamon.
3. Add date molasses and tahini, then mix until a soft, slightly sticky dough forms.
4. Shape into small balls or desired shapes, and roll in sesame seeds if using.
5. Arrange on a lined baking tray and gently flatten if preferred.
6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, until lightly golden and set around the edges.
7. Allow to cool completely before serving. They will firm up and crisp as they cool.
