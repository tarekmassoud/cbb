## Ingredients
- 4 duck breasts  
- Sea salt + pepper  
- 1kg baby potatoes  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 bunch parsley  
- 2 garlic cloves  

## Instructions
1. Wash + halve potatoes; toss with garlic, parsley, oil, salt, pepper.  
2. Preheat tray; roast potatoes 15 minutes.  
3. Score duck skin; season.  
4. Place duck on rack over potatoes; roast 20 minutes.  
5. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing.
