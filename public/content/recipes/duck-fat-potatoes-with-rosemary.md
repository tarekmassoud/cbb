## Ingredients
- 4 kg potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced  
- 300 g duck fat (or 1/4 cup olive oil)  
- 3 tsp salt flakes  
- 1 tsp cracked black pepper  
- 3 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200ÂºC.  
2. Toss potatoes with fat, salt, pepper, and rosemary.  
3. Arrange upright in a dish.  
4. Roast 1 hour until golden and crisp.  
5. Sprinkle with extra rosemary and salt before serving.
