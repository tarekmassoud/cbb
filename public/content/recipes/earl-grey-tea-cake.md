## Ingredients
- 1 cup vegetable oil  
- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 1½ tsp baking powder  
- ½ tsp baking soda  
- 2 large eggs  
- 1¼ cup brown sugar  
- 1 cup Greek yogurt  
- ¼ cup brewed Earl Grey tea  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 tbsp granulated brown sugar (for topping)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C.  
2. Mix flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.  
3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until pale, then add yogurt, tea, and vanilla.  
4. Gradually mix in oil, then fold in dry ingredients until smooth.  
5. Pour batter into a 9x5" loaf pan, sprinkle sugar on top, and bake for 1 hour or until a toothpick comes out clean.
