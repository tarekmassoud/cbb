## Ingredients
### Dough
- 2 tsp dry yeast
- 1 1/2 cups lukewarm milk
- 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp orange zest

### Glaze
- 1/3 cup water
- 1/2 cup sugar

### Icing
- 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
- 2 tbsp milk

## Instructions
1. Activate yeast in milk. Mix dough ingredients 5 minutes.  
2. Rise 1 hour. Divide into 12 balls, proof 30 minutes.  
3. Bake at 180°C for 20 to 25 minutes. Brush with glaze while warm.  
4. Cool, then pipe icing crosses.
