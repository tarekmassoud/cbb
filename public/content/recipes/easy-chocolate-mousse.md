## Ingredients
- 150g mini marshmallows  
- 50g butter  
- 250g dark chocolate, chopped  
- 60ml hot water  
- 284ml double cream  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  

## Instructions
1. Melt marshmallows, butter, chocolate, and water in a saucepan.  
2. Stir until smooth and remove from heat.  
3. Whip cream with vanilla, fold into cooled chocolate mixture.  
4. Spoon into glasses and chill 40 minutes.
