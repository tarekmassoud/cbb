## Ingredients
- 250 g lamb
- 50 g butter
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 10 eggs
- Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook lamb in oil.
2. Add butter.
3. Pour beaten eggs.
4. Cook until set.
5. Serve with saj bread.
