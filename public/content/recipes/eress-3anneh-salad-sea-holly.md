## Ingredients
- 1 bunch of Eress 3anneh (Alpine Sea Holly), leaves washed and trimmed  
- 1 fennel bulb, cut into wedges  
- 1 large red onion, cut into wedges  
- 1 bunch of radishes, sliced  
- 1 kohlrabi, peeled and sliced  
- 1 lemon, cut into small pieces  
- 1 head of red cabbage, thinly sliced (1/4 head)  
- 1 head of green cabbage, thinly sliced (1/4 head)  
- 5 tbsp apple cider vinegar  
- 3 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt to taste

## Instructions
1. In a large salad bowl, whisk together the cider vinegar, olive oil, and a pinch of salt until combined.  
2. Add the Eress 3anneh, fennel wedges, red onion wedges, sliced radishes, kohlrabi, red cabbage, and green cabbage.  
3. Gently toss to coat all the ingredients evenly.  
4. Taste and adjust salt if needed. Serve immediately and enjoy the crisp, refreshing flavors.
