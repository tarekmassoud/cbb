## Ingredients
- Mixed greens (spinach, arugula, basil)
- 1 small sweet potato, roasted
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 2 cups cooked black rice
- Grilled halloumi or feta, optional
- 1 small red onion, cut into wedges
- Pumpkin seeds
- 2 or 3 poached eggs

### Dressing
- ½ an avocado
- 1 garlic clove
- Juice and finely grated zest of 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp honey or maple
- 1 handful basil and parsley
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup warm water
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
- Roast sweet potato and onion at 200°C until tender. Cook black rice as per packet.
- Blend all dressing ingredients until smooth, thinning with warm water as needed.
- Assemble bowls with greens, rice, vegetables, avocado, cheese if using, and seeds.
- Poach eggs to your liking and set on top. Drizzle with dressing and serve.
