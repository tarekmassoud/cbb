## Ingredients
### Eggplants
- 2 large eggplants
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

### Tahini Yogurt Sauce
- 2 cups plain yogurt
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt, to taste

### Garnish
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp slivered almonds or pine nuts
- 3/4 cup cooked chickpeas
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
- Toasted bread or pita, for serving

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200°C on grill or roast. Prick eggplants and grill or roast until tender. Peel, drain briefly, and lay on a lined baking sheet.  
2. Whisk yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt until smooth.  
3. Sauté nuts in olive oil until golden.  
4. Spoon sauce over eggplants, then top with chickpeas, parsley, pomegranate, and nuts. Serve with toasted bread.
