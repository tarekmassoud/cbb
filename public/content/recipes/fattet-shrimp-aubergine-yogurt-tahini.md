## Ingredients

### For the Shrimp

- 500 g shrimp, peeled and deveined  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- ¼ tsp paprika  
- Salt, to taste  

### For the Yogurt-Tahini Sauce

- 2 cups plain yogurt  
- 1 large tbsp tahini  
- 1 large tbsp lemon juice  
- Salt, to taste  

### For the Aubergine

- 1 medium eggplant, cut into small cubes  
- Olive oil, for brushing or drizzling  
- Salt, to taste  

### For Garnish and Plating

- ½ cup cooked chickpeas  
- 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds  
- 2 tbsp pine nuts, toasted in olive oil  
- Thinly sliced radish, for decoration  
- Crispy toasted Lebanese bread, served on the side  

## Instructions

### Roast the Aubergine

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).  
2. Toss the aubergine cubes with olive oil and salt.  
3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20–25 minutes, until golden and tender. Set aside.  

### Cook the Shrimp

1. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat.  
2. Add the shrimp and sauté for 2–3 minutes, until pink and cooked through.  
3. Season with salt and paprika. Set aside.  

### Yogurt-Tahini Sauce

1. In a bowl, whisk together the yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt until smooth and creamy.  
2. Taste and adjust lemon or salt to your liking.  
3. Fold the roasted aubergine pieces into the sauce.  

### Toast the Pine Nuts

1. In a small pan, heat a drizzle of olive oil.  
2. Add the pine nuts and toast, stirring constantly, until golden.  
3. Remove immediately and set aside.  

### Plating

1. In a shallow serving dish, spread a layer of chickpeas at the base.  
2. Spoon over a generous layer of the yogurt-aubergine sauce.  
3. Arrange the shrimp attractively on top.  
4. Sprinkle with pomegranate seeds, toasted pine nuts, and sliced radish.  
5. Serve immediately with crispy toasted Lebanese bread on the side.
