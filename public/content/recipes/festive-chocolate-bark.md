## Ingredients
- 2 Lindt white chocolate bars
- 2 Lindt dark chocolate bars
- 2 Lindt milk chocolate bars
- 4 Lindt bears
- 4 Lindt Santa chocolates
- A handful of multicolour Lindt chocolate bonbons
- Red small bonbons
- 3 to 4 candy canes, crushed

## Instructions
1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
2. Place all chocolate bars (white, milk, dark) on the tray.
3. Put the tray in a 180Â°C oven for a few minutes, just until the chocolate softens and melts.
4. Using a knife or skewer, gently swirl the chocolates to create a marbled effect.
5. While still warm, decorate with bears, Santa chocolates, bonbons, and crushed candy canes.
6. Let cool completely in the fridge until fully set.
7. Break into pieces and enjoy.
