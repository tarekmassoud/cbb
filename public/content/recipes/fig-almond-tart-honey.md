## Ingredients
- 130 g unsalted butter, softened
- 3/4 cup caster sugar
- 1 tbsp finely grated lemon rind
- 1 tbsp finely grated orange rind
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups almond meal
- 1/2 cup plain flour, sifted
- 1/2 tsp baking powder, sifted
- 1 cup flaked almonds, toasted
- 6–10 figs, quartered or halved
- 1/4 cup honey

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a 28 cm fluted tart tin.
- Beat butter, sugar, rinds, and vanilla until light. Beat in eggs one at a time.
- Fold in almond meal, flour, and baking powder; mix in flaked almonds.
- Spread into tin. Arrange figs on top; press slightly.
- Bake 45 minutes until golden. Brush with warm honey while hot.
