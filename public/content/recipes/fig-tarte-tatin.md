## Ingredients
- 1× 375 g puff pastry, thawed
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 vanilla bean, split (or 1 tsp extract)
- 1/4 cup honey
- 50 g butter
- 24 figs, halved

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 190°C. Roll pastry to 5 mm thick; chill.
- Cook sugar, water, and vanilla until dissolved; increase heat and cook 7–9 minutes to light amber. Remove vanilla.
- Stir in honey and butter. Pour into a greased 22–24 cm round tin.
- Arrange figs cut side down; top with pastry, tucking edges in.
- Bake 25–27 minutes until puffed and golden. Invert onto a plate; serve warm.
