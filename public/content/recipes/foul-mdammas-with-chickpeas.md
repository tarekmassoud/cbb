## Ingredients

- 3 cans fava beans, drained  
- 3 cans chickpeas, drained  
- 5–7 garlic cloves, crushed to a paste with salt  
- Juice of 1 lemon (or to taste)  
- Extra-virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Warm favas and chickpeas separately in water for 10–15 minutes; drain.  
2. In a serving bowl, mix the garlic paste with lemon juice and a good drizzle of olive oil.  
3. Roughly mash half the warm beans; fold in the rest.  
4. Season to taste with more lemon, salt, and olive oil.  
5. Serve warm with bread and traditional sides (pickles, mint, radishes).
