## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups cooked freekeh
- 2 cups roasted pumpkin, cut into small cubes
- 1 avocado, diced
- 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup almonds, roughly chopped
- 2 tbsp sultanas
- 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds

### Dressing
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp pomegranate molasses
- A pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine cooked freekeh and roasted pumpkin.
2. Add avocado, pomegranate seeds, parsley, almonds, sultanas, and pumpkin seeds.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, vinegar, olive oil, pomegranate molasses, and salt.
4. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
5. Serve fresh.
