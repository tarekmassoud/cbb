## Ingredients

### Dough
- 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp instant yeast
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 1/2 cups warm milk (about 43°C)
- 1 egg, room temperature
- 2 tbsp melted butter (about 28g)

### Garlic Parmesan Butter Glaze
- 8 tbsp butter, melted (about 112g)
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl (or mixer bowl), combine flour, instant yeast, and salt.
2. Add warm milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until a dough forms.
3. Knead 8 to 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
4. Cover and let rise until doubled in size.
5. Divide into 26 equal pieces and roll each into a smooth ball.
6. Arrange on a lined tray in a turkey shape (a larger cluster for the body, smaller for wings and head).
7. Let rise again until puffy.
8. Bake at 180°C until golden (about 18 to 25 minutes, depending on your oven).
9. Mix melted butter with garlic, Italian seasoning, and parmesan.
10. Brush generously over the warm bread before serving.
