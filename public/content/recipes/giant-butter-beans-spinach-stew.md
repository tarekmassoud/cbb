## Ingredients

- 2 packs giant butter beans from Plein Soleil
- 10 celery stalks, small cubes
- 4 carrots, cubed
- 2 onions, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp oregano
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 chili
- 1 liter stock
- 1 small cup basmati rice
- 1 bunch baby spinach
- Olive oil, for cooking and finishing

## Instructions

1. Place the giant beans in hot water, boil for 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.
2. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil and add the onions, carrots, and celery together. Sauté until slightly softened.
3. Add the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
4. Stir in the tomato paste, paprika, and salt, and let them cook for a minute.
5. Add the drained beans and pour in the stock. Add the bay leaves and chili. Bring to a boil.
6. Add the basmati rice, lower the heat, cover, and let it simmer until the rice and vegetables are fully cooked.
7. Add the baby spinach at the end and cook just until wilted.
8. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil and serve hot.
