## Ingredients
- 2 × 700g walnut baguettes, sliced  
- 420g caramelized onion relish  
- 200g goat’s curd or soft goat cheese  
- 1 bunch rosemary  
- 1.25 liters pouring cream  
- 6 eggs  
- Salt + black pepper  
- 2/3 cup grated parmesan  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  
2. Arrange bread in a large deep baking tray.  
3. Spoon caramelized onions, goat cheese, and rosemary over top.  
4. Whisk eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and parmesan.  
5. Pour mixture over bread.  
6. Allow to soak 10 minutes.  
7. Bake 35 minutes or until golden.
