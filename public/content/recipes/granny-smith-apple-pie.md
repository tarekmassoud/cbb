## Ingredients

### For the Dough
- 3 cups plain flour  
- 125g caster sugar  
- 225g cold chopped butter  
- 4 tbsp iced water  

### For the Filling
- 16 Granny Smith apples, peeled and chopped  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 2 tbsp water  
- 1/2 cup lemon juice  
- 1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon  
- 1 cup almond meal  
- 1 egg yolk (for brushing)  
- Brown sugar (sprinkling)

## Instructions
1. Process flour, sugar, and butter; add iced water until dough forms.  
2. Wrap and chill 30 minutes.  
3. Preheat oven to 190°C.  
4. Cook apples with water and lemon until tender; cool.  
5. Stir in sugar and cinnamon.  
6. Divide dough: 2/3 and 1/3.  
7. Roll out base, place in 24 cm tin.  
8. Sprinkle with almond meal.  
9. Add apples, top with remaining pastry.  
10. Brush with egg, sprinkle with sugar.  
11. Bake 30 minutes or until golden.
