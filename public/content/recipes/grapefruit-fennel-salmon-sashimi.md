## Ingredients
- 1 large pink grapefruit  
- Juice of ½ lemon  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 tsp maple syrup  
- 1 small red chili, sliced  
- ½ fennel bulb, thinly sliced  
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced  
- 200 g ruby red salmon sashimi  
- 1 tbsp dill leaves  
- 2 tbsp pomegranate arils  
- ¼ cup Greek yogurt  
- 3 tbsp salmon roe

## Instructions
1. Peel and segment the grapefruit, reserving extra juice.  
2. In a bowl, whisk grapefruit juice, lemon juice, olive oil, maple syrup, chili, and salt.  
3. Toss fennel and onion with dressing.  
4. Arrange salmon slices on a platter, top with salad, pomegranate, yogurt, and roe.  
5. Serve immediately.
