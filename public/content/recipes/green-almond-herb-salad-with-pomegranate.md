## Ingredients

- Green almonds, thinly sliced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cucumbers, diced
- Pomegranate seeds
- Green za’atar leaves
- Fresh mint leaves
- Fresh parsley leaves
- 1/2 lemon, finely diced, peel included
- Walnuts, roughly chopped
- Pumpkin seeds
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Fresh lemon juice
- Sea salt

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine all the fresh ingredients: green almonds, onions, cucumber, herbs, pomegranate seeds, and lemon.
2. Add walnuts and pumpkin seeds for crunch.
3. Drizzle generously with olive oil and fresh lemon juice.
4. Season with sea salt.
5. Mix everything very well until fully combined.
6. Taste and adjust lemon, salt, or olive oil if needed.
