## Ingredients

- 1 kg green beans
- 1/2 kg lamb shank meat
- 1 cup water
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 medium onions, sliced
- 1 large onion, peeled and diced
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 250 ml tomato juice
- 1 tsp tomato paste
- Additional salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup of water (extra for simmering)

## Instructions

1. In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté the diced onion until slightly golden.
2. Add the lamb and cook until browned.
3. Pour in 1 cup of water and simmer until the meat is tender.
4. Mix tomato juice, paste, water, salt, and pepper; add to the pot.
5. Trim beans, halve lengthwise, rinse well; add to pot.
6. Cover and simmer 15–20 minutes, until beans are tender.
7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
