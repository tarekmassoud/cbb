## Ingredients
- 200g penne or orecchiette  
- Grated parmesan or pecorino cheese  
- 3/4 cup frozen peas  
- A handful of fresh basil leaves  
- 2 tbsp toasted sunflower seeds  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 clove garlic  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper  

## Instructions
1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.  
2. Blend all pesto ingredients until smooth, adjusting with olive oil or warm water for consistency.  
3. Toss with pasta, sprinkle with cheese, and serve immediately.
