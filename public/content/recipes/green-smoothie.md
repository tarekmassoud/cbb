## Ingredients

- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup fresh pineapple
- 1/2 cup frozen pineapple
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 small carrot
- 1/2 avocado
- A handful of fresh spinach
- A small piece of ginger, to taste
- 1 tbsp organic honey
- A splash of water or milk, as needed

## Instructions

1. Add all the ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. If needed, add a splash of water or milk to reach your desired consistency.
4. Serve immediately.
