## Ingredients

### Marinade
- 1 kg baby calamari  
- 3 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1/2 cup olive oil  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1/4 tsp black pepper  
- 1/2 tsp paprika  
- 1/2 tsp chili pepper  
- Juice of one large lemon

## Instructions
1. Marinate the calamari in the mixture and refrigerate for 1–2 hours.  
2. Preheat a plancha or grill pan. Grill the calamari for 5 minutes on each side.  
3. Prepare a fresh salad with scallions and radishes, toss with oil, lemon, and salt, and add the grilled calamari on top.
