## Ingredients
- Grilled chicken, sliced  
- 2 avocados, sliced  
- 1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced  
- 1 cup mozzarella, grated  
- 1 cup sweet corn  
- Romaine or mixed leaves

### Dressing
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard  
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise or whole-milk yogurt  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Juice of 1/2 lemon  
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed  
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Whisk dressing until smooth and emulsified.  
2. Arrange salad ingredients on a platter.  
3. Drizzle with dressing, season to taste, and serve immediately.
