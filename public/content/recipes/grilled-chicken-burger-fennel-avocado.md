## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
- 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 6 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup chopped cornichon
- Sea salt and black pepper
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 4 burger buns, split
- 1 small fennel bulb, thinly sliced
- 1 cup basil leaves
- 1 head sucrine or small romaine, leaves separated
- 1 medium tomato, sliced
- 1–2 avocados, sliced
- Olive oil

## Instructions
1. Whisk mustard, tamari, garlic, lemon juice, and pepper. Reserve 1/3 cup; whisk with mayonnaise and cornichon for dressing.
2. Toss chicken with remaining marinade; rest 30 minutes.
3. Grill chicken over medium heat until charred and cooked through. Toast buns.
4. Spread dressing on buns. Layer lettuce, tomato, avocado, chicken, fennel, and basil. Serve immediately.
