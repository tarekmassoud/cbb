## Ingredients

### For the Marinade
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
- 2 tbsp + 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
- 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil (plus more for grilling)
- Sea salt and black pepper

### For the Burger
- 4 large (or 6 small) skinless boneless chicken or turkey breasts
- 4 burger buns
- 1 head sucrine (or crisp lettuce)
- 1 medium tomato, sliced into rounds
- 4 slices avocado

### For the Lemon Mayo and Salad
- 6 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup chopped cornichons
- 1 medium fennel bulb, thinly sliced
- 1 cup basil leaves

## Instructions
1. Whisk Dijon, Worcestershire, garlic, 2 tbsp lemon juice, black pepper, and olive oil. Season with salt.
2. Transfer half the marinade to a small bowl. Mix it with mayonnaise and cornichons. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.
3. Add chicken breasts to the remaining marinade, toss to coat, and let sit at least 30 minutes.
4. Grill chicken until well charred and cooked through.
5. Toss the fennel with basil and the remaining 2 tsp lemon juice (add a drizzle of olive oil if needed). Season with salt and pepper.
6. Spread lemon mayo on the buns. Add lettuce, grilled chicken, tomato, avocado, and top with fennel-basil salad.
