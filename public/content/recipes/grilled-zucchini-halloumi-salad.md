## Ingredients
- 2 green zucchini, cut lengthwise  
- 2 yellow zucchini, cut lengthwise  
- 1 yellow capsicum, seeded and sliced  
- 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved  
- 200 g halloumi, thick slices  
- 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp butter  
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar  
- Basil leaves, salt, cracked pepper

## Instructions
1. Grill zucchini and capsicum with olive oil, salt, and pepper until charred and tender.  
2. Pan-grill halloumi with butter until golden.  
3. Toss tomatoes with vinegar, olive oil, and seasonings.  
4. Combine all on a platter, scatter basil, and serve.
