## Ingredients
- 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar  
- 1 tsp honey  
- 4 tbsp olive oil, plus more to coat zucchini  
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano  
- 1/2 cup jar capers, drained and roughly chopped  
- 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted and roughly chopped  
- Kosher salt  
- Freshly cracked black pepper  
- 1 1/2 lbs zucchini, sliced 1/2-inch thick (≈2 large)  
- 1 cup whole-milk ricotta cheese  
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves

## Instructions
1. Whisk balsamic and honey. Drizzle in olive oil; add oregano, capers, and walnuts. Season with salt and pepper.  
2. Heat a grill or grill pan. Coat zucchini slices with olive oil and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.  
3. Grill 3–4 minutes per side until charred and tender.  
4. Arrange on a platter, add dollops of ricotta, and drizzle the caper-walnut vinaigrette.  
5. Garnish with basil and/or mint. Serve warm or at room temp.
