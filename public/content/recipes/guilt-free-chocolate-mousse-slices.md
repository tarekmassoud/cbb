## Ingredients

### Base
- 1 1/2 cups rolled oats  
- 1 cup desiccated coconut  
- 1 cup almond meal  
- 300g dates, pitted  
- 80g melted butter  
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder  

### Mousse
- 2 cans coconut cream (chilled)  
- 1/3 cup cocoa powder  
- 1/4 cup coconut sugar  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C.  
2. Process base ingredients until combined; press into a tin and bake 20 min.  
3. Cool completely.  
4. Whisk chilled coconut cream with cocoa and sugar.  
5. Spread over base and refrigerate until set.
