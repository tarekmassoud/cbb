## Ingredients
### Filling
- 250 g halloumi, diced
- 4 spring onions, chopped
- 1 red chili, finely chopped
- 2 plum tomatoes, chopped
- Handful coriander, chopped
- 2 tsp harissa or chili jam
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 250 g ready-cooked freekeh
- Salt and pepper

### Peppers
- 6 mixed bell peppers, halved lengthwise and deseeded
- 1 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 190°C. Toss pepper halves with oil and season. Roast cut-side up 15 minutes.  
2. Mix halloumi, spring onions, chili, tomatoes, coriander, harissa, olive oil, garlic, and freekeh. Season.  
3. Fill peppers with mixture. Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 10 minutes more until hot and lightly browned.
