## Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados, mashed  
- 1 small red onion, finely chopped  
- Fresh lemon juice  
- Salt & pepper  
- Halloumi slices  
- 2 eggs, poached

## Instructions

1. Mix avocado, onion, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make guacamole.  
2. Grill halloumi in a dry pan until golden on both sides.  
3. Stack: halloumi + guacamole + poached egg. Serve immediately.
