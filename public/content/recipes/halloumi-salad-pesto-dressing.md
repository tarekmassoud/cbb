## Ingredients
- Halloumi
- Rocket
- Cucumber
- Carrots
- Mushrooms
- Pesto
- Lemon juice
- Olive oil

## Instructions
1. Grill halloumi until golden.
2. Toss vegetables together.
3. Whisk pesto, lemon and olive oil.
4. Combine and serve.
