## Ingredients
### Potatoes
- 5–6 medium sweet potatoes  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Sea salt and pepper  
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan

### Salsa verde
- 1 cup parsley leaves  
- 1 bunch basil leaves  
- 1 clove garlic  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice  
- 2–3 tbsp olive oil  
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C. Slice potatoes three-quarters of the way through at 3 mm intervals. Place on a lined tray.  
2. Drizzle with oil, season, and roast 40 minutes until tender. Sprinkle parmesan on top; roast 5 minutes more.  
3. Blitz salsa verde ingredients to a coarse sauce.  
4. Spoon salsa over potatoes and garnish with extra basil.
