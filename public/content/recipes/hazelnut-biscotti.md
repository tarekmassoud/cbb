## Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 250 g flour
- 180 g brown sugar + extra for rolling
- Pinch of salt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 25 g butter, melted
- 175 g roasted hazelnuts

## Instructions
- Mix eggs, sugar, butter, salt, and baking powder; add flour to form a dough. Fold in hazelnuts.
- Chill 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 190°C.
- Divide in two; roll into 3 cm × 30 cm logs. Roll in sugar; place on tray.
- Bake 25 minutes. Cool 10 minutes; slice 1 cm thick on the diagonal.
- Return to tray, cut side down; bake 10 minutes more.
