## Ingredients

- 5 rows of soft dates (about 25 dates)
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 2 bananas, sliced
- 300 g dark chocolate
- 1 tsp coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Remove the pits from the dates and place them tightly next to each other in a lined tray or container.
2. Press the dates well using your hands or the bottom of a glass until you get one compact layer.
3. Spread the peanut butter evenly over the dates.
4. Add the sliced bananas on top.
5. Melt the dark chocolate with the coconut oil until smooth and glossy.
6. Pour the melted chocolate over the bananas and spread evenly.
7. Refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours until firm.
8. Cut into triangles and enjoy.
