## Ingredients

- 600 g (5 cups) all-purpose flour
- 437 g (1 3/4 cups + 1 tbsp) water
- 12 g (2 tsp) salt
- 2 g (3/4 tsp) yeast

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix until a rough dough forms.
2. Fold the dough over itself, bringing it up from the bottom while rotating the bowl.
3. Cover and let the dough rest at room temperature for 3 hours.
4. During this time, every 30 minutes, gently fold the dough. It will become smoother, stronger, and more elastic.
5. Divide the dough into 3 equal pieces, about 350 g each. Lightly shape into balls and let them rest briefly.
6. Shape each piece into baguettes.
7. Cover and let rest at room temperature for about 1 hour, until slightly puffy.
8. Preheat the oven to 260°C.
9. Transfer the dough onto a hot baking stone.
10. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until golden brown. If they brown too quickly, slightly lower the temperature.
