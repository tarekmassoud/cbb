## Ingredients
- 1 cup toasted pine nuts  
- 4 tbsp (1/4 cup) lemon juice  
- 2 small garlic cloves  
- 1/2 tsp salt, and pepper to taste  
- 4 big bunches basil leaves  
- 1/2 cup olive oil, plus more if needed  
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

## Instructions
1. Add pine nuts, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper to a processor.  
2. Add basil and pulse. With the processor running, slowly stream in olive oil.  
3. Mix in Parmesan. Add a little extra oil for a looser texture if desired.  
4. Store in small jars; freeze for up to 6 months.
