## Ingredients
- 400 g minced beef  
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper  
- 2 brioche burger buns, halved  
- 1 cup shredded cabbage  
- 1/2 cup caramelized onions  
- Heinz mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup (to taste)  
- Olive oil, for cooking

## Instructions
1. Season beef, form 2–4 patties. Heat a pan and cook 3–4 min per side.  
2. Toast buns. Mix cabbage with a little mayo.  
3. Spread sauces on buns, add onions and patties.  
4. Assemble and serve hot.
