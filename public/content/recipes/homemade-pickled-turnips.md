## Ingredients
- 1 kg small white turnips, peeled and cut into sticks
- 1 small beet, sliced
- 2 cups water
- 2 cups white vinegar
- 2 tbsp coarse salt
- 2 garlic cloves

## Instructions
1. Sterilize a jar with hot water and let dry.
2. Place turnips, beet slices, and garlic in the jar.
3. Heat water, vinegar, and salt until dissolved.
4. Pour over vegetables until fully submerged.
