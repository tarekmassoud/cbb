## Ingredients
- 1 kg fresh milk  
- 2–3 tbsp plain yogurt (starter)

## Instructions
1. Bring milk to a boil; let cool until warm (not hot).  
2. Stir in yogurt starter until combined.  
3. Cover and keep in a warm spot 6–8 hours (or overnight for thicker).  
4. Chill to set. Enjoy plain, with honey, or in dishes.
