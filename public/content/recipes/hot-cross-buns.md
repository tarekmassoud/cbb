## Ingredients
### Dough
- 2 tsp dry yeast
- 1 1/2 cups lukewarm milk
- 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp orange zest
- 1/2 cup raisins

### Sugar Glaze
- 1/3 cup water
- 1/2 cup sugar

### Icing Cross
- 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
- 2 tbsp milk

## Instructions
1. Sprinkle yeast over warm milk and let stand 5 minutes.  
2. In a stand mixer combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, butter, egg, and orange zest. Pour in yeast mixture and mix 5 minutes until combined.  
3. Cover and let rise 1 hour.  
4. Punch down and turn onto a lightly floured surface. Knead 1 to 2 minutes. Divide into 12 equal portions, shape into balls, place on a lined tray, cover, and proof 30 minutes.  
5. Bake at 180°C for 20 to 25 minutes until golden.  
6. For the glaze, simmer water and sugar 5 minutes, then brush over warm buns. Cool completely.  
7. Whisk confectioners’ sugar with milk, pipe crosses, and set.
