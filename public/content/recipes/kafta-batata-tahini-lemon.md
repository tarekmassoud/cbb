## Ingredients
### Kafta
- 500 g ground lamb or beef
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- Handful parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp seven-spice

### Tahini Lemon Sauce
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 1/3 cup lemon juice
- 1/2 cup water
- Salt, to taste

### Other
- 1 large onion, sliced into ribbons
- 6 medium potatoes, wedged
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Toasted sesame seeds
- 6 crushed garlic cloves mixed with 2 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Mix kafta ingredients and shape into ovals. Sear in a skillet or on a grill pan.  
2. Toss potatoes and onions with oil and season. Roast at 180°C until halfway cooked.  
3. Whisk tahini with lemon juice and water until smooth.  
4. Arrange kafta, potatoes, and onions in a dish. Drizzle tahini sauce and garlic oil. Bake 20 minutes, then sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.
