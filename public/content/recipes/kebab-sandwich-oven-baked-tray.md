## Ingredients

### For the Kebab

- 1 kg ground lamb
- 1 white onion, very finely chopped or grated
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp Aleppo pepper or cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp salt

### For the Sandwich

- Hummus
- 1 onion, sliced, mixed with sumac and a pinch of salt
- Rocca (arugula)
- Tomato wedges
- Bread (pita or saj)

## Instructions

### Prepare the Kebab

1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, onion, garlic, parsley, tomato paste, and all the spices.
3. Mix well using your hands until everything is fully incorporated.
4. Transfer the mixture to a baking tray.
5. Press it down evenly to form a flat layer about 1.5 to 2 cm thick.
6. Score lightly with a knife into squares or strips.
7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until fully cooked and slightly browned on top.
8. For extra color, broil for 2 to 3 minutes at the end.

### Assemble the Sandwich

1. Spread a layer of hummus.
2. Add pieces of the baked kebab.
3. Top with sumac onions, rocca, and tomato wedges.
4. Close, wrap, and enjoy.
