## Ingredients

### Kebbeh Shell
- 2 cups fine bulgur, washed and soaked 1 cup hot water  
- 500 g ground lamb or beef  
- 1 large onion  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1/2 tsp black pepper  
- 50 g butter + drizzle olive oil

### Stuffing
- 200 g ground lamb  
- 1/2 cup pine nuts  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1/2 tsp pepper

## Instructions
1. Process onion to a paste; mix with bulgur and half the meat in a processor until combined. Remove; add salt and pepper; knead like a dough.  
2. For stuffing, sauté ground meat in a non-stick pan with a drizzle of oil; add pine nuts; cook 5–6 minutes. Set aside.  
3. Heat oven to 200°C. Oil a tray.  
4. Divide kebbeh into two parts. Press one part evenly into tray. Spread stuffing over; top with remaining kebbeh, smoothing the surface.  
5. Score diamond patterns, drizzle with butter and olive oil. Bake ~40 minutes until top is well browned. Serve with laban/ayran and chopped parsley.
