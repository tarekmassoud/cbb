## Ingredients
- 1 big bunch Khebbayzeh (wild herb/greens)  
- 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked and peeled (or cooked chickpeas)  
- 2 large onions, finely chopped  
- 4 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt to taste  
- Lemon juice, to serve

## Instructions
1. Soak the dried chickpeas in water overnight. Drain, rinse, and remove the skins.  
2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until translucent.  
3. Add the chickpeas to the pan and stir to combine. Cook for another 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.  
4. Add the Khebbayzeh (greens) and salt to taste. Stir well to mix everything.  
5. Cover and cook for around 15 minutes or until the Khebbayzeh is fully cooked through.  
6. Squeeze lemon juice over the dish and serve warm with Lebanese bread.
