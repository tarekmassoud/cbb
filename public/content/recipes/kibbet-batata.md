## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg potatoes
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup fine bulgur, washed and well drained
- 1/4 cup walnuts, finely chopped
- A handful of walnuts, roughly chopped, for crunch
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Fresh mint leaves, for garnish
- Extra olive oil, for serving

## Instructions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes into chunks.
2. Place them in salted boiling water and cook until tender.
3. Drain the potatoes and mash them while still hot until smooth.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. In a bowl, mix the sliced onion with a pinch of salt and pepper, and gently rub to soften.
6. Add the olive oil to the mashed potatoes and mix well.
7. Add the drained bulgur and the finely chopped walnuts, then mix thoroughly.
8. Add the onion mixture to the potatoes and combine everything well until fully incorporated.
9. Fold in the roughly chopped walnuts for extra texture.
10. Transfer to a serving plate and shape as desired.
11. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and drizzle with olive oil before serving.
