## Ingredients

### Meringue
- 225ml egg whites (about 6 eggs), room temperature  
- 330g caster sugar  
- 1½ tsp white vinegar  

### Topping
- 1 cup single cream  
- ½ cup sour cream  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 5 kiwis, sliced  
- 1 tbsp chopped pistachio  

### Citrus Syrup
- ¼ cup caster sugar  
- ¼ cup lime or grapefruit juice  
- 5cm piece fresh ginger  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Whisk egg whites until stiff peaks form.  
2. Gradually add sugar, whisking until glossy. Add vinegar and whisk briefly.  
3. Spoon onto baking paper, shaping into a round.  
4. Bake 1½ hours, then turn oven off and cool inside.  
5. Simmer syrup ingredients until thickened, cool completely.  
6. Whip creams with vanilla. Top pavlova with cream, kiwi, pistachio, and drizzle syrup.
