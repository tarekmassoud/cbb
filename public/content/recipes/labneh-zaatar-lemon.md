## Ingredients
### Labneh
- 1 L Lebanese yogurt
- 1/3 cup pitted green olives, finely chopped
- 2 tsp lemon zest
- Pinch of salt

### Topping
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/3 cup Lebanese za’atar
- 2 tbsp shredded lemon zest
- 1/3 tsp cracked black pepper
- Lebanese flatbread, toasted, for serving

## Instructions
1. Mix yogurt, olives, and lemon zest with a pinch of salt. Line a colander with cheesecloth and pour in the yogurt. Gather edges and tie.  
2. Set over a bowl and refrigerate 24 to 72 hours to strain until thick.  
3. Spoon onto a platter. Heat olive oil in a pan, add za’atar, shredded zest, and pepper for 30 seconds, then cool slightly.  
4. Spoon the mixture over the labneh and serve with flatbreads.
