## Ingredients
### Semolina Base
- 7 cups milk
- 10 tbsp corn flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

### Whipped Layer
- 2 cups whipping cream
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 1 packet banana-flavored pudding or essence (optional)

### Syrup and Topping
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp orange blossom water
- 1 tsp rose water
- 1/2 cup ground pistachio
- 1/2 cup slivered almonds

## Instructions
1. For the base, whisk milk, corn flour, sugar, and vanilla over medium heat until thick. Spread in a dish and cool.  
2. Whip cream with milk and vanilla, then fold in banana flavor if using. Spread over the chilled base.  
3. Simmer water and sugar 5 minutes, add lemon juice and floral waters, then cool.  
4. Chill dessert a few hours. Serve with syrup and scatter pistachio and almonds.
