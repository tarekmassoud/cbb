## Ingredients
**For the dressing**
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil  
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed  
- 1/4 cup verjuice  
- Sea salt, to taste

**Salad**
- 800 g loubieh khadra (green beans)  
- Cherry tomatoes, to top  
- 3 boiled potatoes, cubed (optional)

## Instructions
1. Bring salted water to a boil. Blanch green beans 8–10 minutes until tender-crisp; drain and cool.  
2. Whisk olive oil, garlic, verjuice, and salt.  
3. Toss beans with dressing. Top with cherry tomatoes and optional potatoes. Adjust salt and serve.
