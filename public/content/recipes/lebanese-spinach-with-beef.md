## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch fresh spinach, chopped  
- 150 g ground beef  
- 2 medium onions, diced  
- Olive oil  
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon  
- A handful of pine nuts  
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Sauté onions in olive oil until soft and translucent.  
2. Add ground beef; cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.  
3. Stir in pine nuts and spinach. Cook on low until spinach wilts.  
4. Add lemon zest and juice; simmer briefly and adjust seasoning.  
5. Serve with steamed rice and lemon wedges.
