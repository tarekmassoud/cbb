## Ingredients
### For the filling
- 900g leeks, trimmed  
- Salt and pepper  
- 250ml double cream  
- 2 eggs  

### For the dough
- 300g plain flour  
- 1½ tsp yeast  
- ½ cup lukewarm milk  
- 2 eggs  
- 100g unsalted butter, softened  

## Instructions
1. Mix flour and salt, make a well in the center.  
2. Dissolve yeast in milk, pour into the well, and add eggs.  
3. Knead into a smooth dough, incorporating butter.  
4. Let rise for 3 hours, then knock down.  
5. Sauté leeks in butter, season, and cool.  
6. Spread dough in a tart pan, add leeks, pour cream-egg mixture.  
7. Bake at 200°C for 40–45 minutes until set and golden.
