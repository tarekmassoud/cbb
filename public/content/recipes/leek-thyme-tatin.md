## Ingredients
- 40 g sugar  
- 10 g butter  
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar  
- 500 g leeks, white and light green parts only, cut into rounds  
- 3 sprigs thyme  
- Salt and pepper  
- 1 sheet puff pastry

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200ºC.  
2. Caramelize sugar in pan, add butter and vinegar.  
3. Add leeks, thyme, salt, and pepper; cook 5 min.  
4. Cover with puff pastry, tucking edges down.  
5. Bake 30 min until golden; rest 5 min, invert onto plate to serve.
