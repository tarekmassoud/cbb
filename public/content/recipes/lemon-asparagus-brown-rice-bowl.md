## Ingredients
- 3 courgettes (zucchini), sliced  
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed  
- 1 bunch spring onions, chopped  
- 2 garlic cloves, minced  
- 300 g short-grain brown rice  
- 550 ml vegetable stock  
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon  
- Handful fresh basil leaves  
- 50 g toasted pine nuts  
- Olive oil, sea salt, pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and brown the courgettes; set aside. Sauté asparagus until just tender; set aside.  
2. Add a little oil, then spring onions and garlic; cook 1 minute.  
3. Stir in rice and stock; simmer covered until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.  
4. Return vegetables, add lemon zest and juice, adjust seasoning.  
5. Serve topped with basil and pine nuts.
