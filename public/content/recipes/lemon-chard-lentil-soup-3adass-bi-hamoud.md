## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 medium onions, peeled and chopped  
- 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed  
- 3 medium potatoes, diced small  
- 5 cloves garlic, crushed  
- 1 big bunch Swiss chard, stems and leaves separated, chopped  
- 1 cup cilantro leaves (fresh or frozen), chopped  
- 1/2 cup lemon juice  
- Salt and pepper, plus a pinch of sumac

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large pot; sauté onions 4–5 minutes.  
2. Add lentils and 4–5 cups water; simmer until starting to soften.  
3. Stir in potatoes; cook until tender.  
4. Add chard stems first for 5 minutes, then leaves and cook just until wilted.  
5. Turn off heat; add cilantro, crushed garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.  
6. Serve warm with a pinch of sumac and crusty bread.
