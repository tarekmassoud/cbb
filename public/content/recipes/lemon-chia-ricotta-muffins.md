## Ingredients
### Muffins
- 375 g self-raising flour  
- 200 g caster sugar  
- Zest of 1 lemon  
- 2 tbsp chia seeds  
- 240 g ricotta  
- 2 eggs  
- 1 tsp vanilla  
- 1 cup Greek yogurt  
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

### Glaze
- 1 cup icing sugar  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- Milk as needed

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 180°C. Line a 12-cup muffin tin.  
2. Whisk flour, sugar, lemon zest, and chia.  
3. In another bowl, whisk ricotta, eggs, vanilla, yogurt, oil, and lemon juice.  
4. Fold wet into dry until just combined. Spoon into cups.  
5. Bake 20–25 minutes until a skewer comes out clean. Cool.  
6. Mix glaze and drizzle over muffins. Sprinkle extra chia if you like.
