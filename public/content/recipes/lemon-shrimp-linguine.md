## Ingredients
- 250 g linguine or thin pasta
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 5 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 15 medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 large lemon, zest and juice
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- Salt and cracked black pepper
- Parmesan, freshly grated, to serve

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.  
2. Sauté garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil and butter until fragrant. Add shrimp and cook 3 to 4 minutes.  
3. Add drained pasta with 1/4 cup reserved water and toss. Stir in lemon juice and zest and parsley.  
4. Season and add more water if needed. Serve with parmesan.
