## Ingredients

### For the Roasted Vegetables

- Cauliflower
- Eggplant
- Zucchini
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper

### For the Filling

- Thinly sliced onion
- Sumac
- Salt
- Radishes
- Cherry tomatoes
- Nayef pickle tarator
- Salad leaves
- Green onions
- Saj bread or your favorite bread

## Instructions

### Roast the Vegetables

1. Slice the vegetables, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and black pepper, and roast for 20 minutes at 180°C.

### Prepare the Filling

1. Mix the thinly sliced onion with salt and sumac.
2. Prepare the radishes, cherry tomatoes, tarator, salad leaves, and green onions.

### Assemble

1. Layer everything in saj bread or your favorite bread.
2. Serve fresh.
