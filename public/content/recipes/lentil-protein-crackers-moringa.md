## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup raw lentils (any variety)
- 60 g mixed seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, flax; about 1/2 cup)
- Boiling water for soaking
- 1.5 cups water
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp moringa powder

## Instructions

1. Soak lentils and seeds in boiling water for 20 minutes; drain.
2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment.
3. Blend drained lentils with 1 1/2 cups fresh water until smooth. Add baking powder, za’atar if using, and salt. Blend again. Add moringa and blend once more.
4. Spread batter evenly on the sheet and bake 20 minutes. Score, then bake until crisp (about 10 minutes more). Cool and break apart.
