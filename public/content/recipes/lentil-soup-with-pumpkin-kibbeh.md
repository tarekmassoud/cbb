## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 2 tsp garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, finely diced
- 2 celery stalks, finely diced
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 cup lentils
- 1/2 cup bulgur
- 8 cups vegetable broth
- Salt, to taste
- A handful of parsley, roughly chopped
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 pack pumpkin kibbeh

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add the olive oil.
2. Sauté the onion until soft and golden.
3. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
4. Stir in the carrot, celery, lentils, bulgur, and vegetable broth.
5. Bring to a boil.
6. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 30 to 40 minutes, until the lentils and bulgur are fully cooked and the vegetables are tender.
7. Season with salt, pepper, cumin, coriander, and cardamom. Mix well.
8. Add the chopped parsley and lemon juice, and stir to combine.
9. Serve hot, with pumpkin kibbeh on the side if desired.
