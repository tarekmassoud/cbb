## Ingredients
- 2 cups macaroni
- 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 250 g speck (bacon), chopped
- 250 ml milk
- 240 g mascarpone
- 130 g cheddar, grated (divided)
- 2 tbsp rosemary leaves
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Boil macaroni until al dente; drain.
2. Heat oil; cook speck until crisp. Remove.
3. In the same pan, add pasta, milk, mascarpone, salt, pepper, and half the cheddar; stir until warm.
4. Transfer to an ovenproof dish. Top with speck, rosemary, and remaining cheddar. Bake 10 minutes or until golden. Serve hot.
