## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup chickpeas
- 1/2 cup kidney beans
- 1/2 cup black-eyed peas
- 1/2 cup white beans
- 1/2 cup lentils
- 1/4 cup rice
- 2 onions
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp turmeric
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Soak beans overnight.
2. Boil until partially tender.
