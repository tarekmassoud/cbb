## Ingredients
- 1 cup almond meal  
- 1 cup flaked almonds  
- 2 egg whites  
- 1/4 cup sugar  
- 3 pears, halved and cored  
- 3 tbsp maple syrup  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  
2. Mix almond meal, flaked almonds, egg whites, and sugar.  
3. Press into a baking tray, bake 10 min.  
4. Slice pears thinly and arrange on crust.  
5. Brush with maple syrup and bake 15 min more.  
6. Serve with ice cream.
