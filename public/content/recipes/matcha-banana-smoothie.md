## Ingredients
- 1Â½ cups almond milk  
- 2 ripe bananas  
- 1 ripe avocado  
- 1 cup baby spinach  
- 2 tsp matcha powder  
- 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds (plus extra for topping)  
- 1 tbsp honey

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.  
2. Adjust consistency with more milk if desired.  
3. Pour into glasses, top with pumpkin seeds, and enjoy chilled.
