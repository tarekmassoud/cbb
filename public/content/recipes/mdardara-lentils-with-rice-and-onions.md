## Ingredients
- 2 cups lentils, rinsed and drained  
- 1/2 cup long grain rice  
- 3 large onions, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced into half moons  
- 2 large brown onions, peeled and cut  
- Olive oil  
- Sea salt and black pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook lentils in water for 30 minutes, or until cooked but still firm; drain, keeping the stock aside.  
2. Cook the rice in salted water until cooked but still firm.  
3. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a medium pan and cook the onion until translucent; add the rice and lentils, and 1/2 cup of the lentil stock.  
4. Put the half-moon onions on a baking tray lined with butter paper, drizzle with olive oil, and grill at 190°C for about 15 minutes until brown and caramelized, stirring often.  
5. Transfer the mdardara onto a serving platter and garnish with the caramelized onion.  
6. Decorate with radish, cucumber, tomato, and pickles.
