## Ingredients
- 1 aubergine, diced  
- 1 courgette, sliced  
- 500g short pasta (penne rigate)  
- 340g arrabbiata sauce  
- 150g Kashkawan cheese, grated  
- Handful fresh basil leaves  
- Sun-dried tomatoes, for serving  
- Salt, to taste  
- Vegetable oil, for sautéing

## Instructions
1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil; cook pasta until al dente.  
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large pan; sauté aubergine until golden and tender.  
3. Add courgette and cook 5 minutes; season with salt. Drain any excess oil.  
4. Stir in arrabbiata sauce and half the basil; simmer 2–3 minutes.  
5. Add cooked pasta and cheese; toss to coat.  
6. Serve topped with sun-dried tomatoes and remaining basil.
