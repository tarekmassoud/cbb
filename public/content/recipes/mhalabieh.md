## Ingredients
- 1 litre milk
- 7 tbsp cornflour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tbsp rose water
- Pistachios

## Instructions
1. Mix milk, cornflour and sugar in a pot.
2. Cook while whisking until thick.
3. Stir in rose water.
4. Pour into bowls and chill before serving.
