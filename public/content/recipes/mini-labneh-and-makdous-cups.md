## Ingredients

- 4 mini makdouss (or 2 large makdouss)
- 1 cup labneh
- 1 pack pain de mini (mini bread rounds)
- Olive oil
- Fresh mint leaves

## Instructions

1. Lightly brush the mini bread rounds with olive oil and toast until golden and crisp.
2. In a bowl, mash the makdouss and mix with the labneh until creamy.
3. Spoon the labneh–makdouss mixture into the toasted cups.
4. Drizzle with a little olive oil and top with fresh mint leaves.
5. Serve immediately as a bite-sized mezze.
