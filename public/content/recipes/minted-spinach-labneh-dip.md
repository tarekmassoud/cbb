## Ingredients
- 500 g baby spinach leaves  
- 1/2 cup almonds, toasted  
- 3 cups mint leaves  
- 1 tbsp grated lemon rind  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- Sea salt and black pepper  
- 1 cup natural Greek yogurt (or Lebanese labneh) + 1 tbsp extra  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Olive and thyme sticks, to serve  
- Extra mint leaves, to decorate

## Instructions
1. Blanch spinach 30 seconds in boiling water, drain and refresh in iced water.  
2. Blend almonds, spinach, mint, lemon rind, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until smooth.  
3. Add yogurt and oil; blend again.  
4. Chill 1 hour before serving.  
5. Serve topped with extra yogurt and mint, alongside olive and thyme sticks.
