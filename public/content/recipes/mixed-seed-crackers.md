## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup sunflower seeds  
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds  
- 1/4 cup sesame seeds  
- 1/4 cup flaxseeds  
- 1/4 cup chia seeds  
- 1/4 cup oregano  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1 cup water

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 150°C and line a baking sheet with parchment.  
2. In a bowl, mix all the seeds, oregano, and salt.  
3. Pour in water and stir thoroughly. Let sit 15 minutes to gel.  
4. Spread thinly and evenly on the tray.  
5. Bake ~1 hour until dry and crisp. Cool completely, then break into crackers.
