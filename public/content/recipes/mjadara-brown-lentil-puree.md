## Ingredients
- 2 cups brown lentils  
- 1/2 cup rice  
- 1 large brown onion  
- 2 tbsp sea salt  
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Wash the lentils and cover with 6 cups of water. Simmer over medium heat until very soft and tender.  
2. Using an immersion blender, grind the lentils and set aside.  
3. In a pan, cook the onion with 2 tbsp olive oil until golden.  
4. Add the rice and water, then cook gently until the rice softens.  
5. Add the ground lentils to the mixture and stir constantly until it thickens slightly.  
6. Season with salt to taste and transfer to a serving dish.
