## Ingredients
- Olive oil  
- 1 large onion, diced  
- 1 eggplant, diced  
- 2 zucchinis, diced  
- 1–2 bell peppers, diced  
- 2 cups tomatoes, diced  
- 1 can chickpeas, drained  
- Salt & pepper, to taste  
- Chili flakes (optional)  
- Ricotta, for topping (optional)

## Instructions
1. Warm olive oil in a wide pan. Sauté onion until translucent.  
2. Add eggplant; cook until beginning to soften.  
3. Stir in zucchini and peppers; cook 3–4 minutes.  
4. Add tomatoes and chickpeas; season generously with salt and pepper (and chili flakes, if using).  
5. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender and flavors meld.  
6. Drizzle with olive oil and serve—optionally dolloped with ricotta.
