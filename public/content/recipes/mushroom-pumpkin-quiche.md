## Ingredients

### Filling
- 600 g butternut pumpkin, thinly sliced  
- 6 portobello mushrooms  
- 150 g pancetta, finely chopped  
- 200 g Taleggio cheese, sliced  
- 2 eggs  
- Salt and pepper

### Pastry
- 300 g plain flour  
- 100 g grated parmesan  
- 150 g cold butter, chopped  
- 1 egg  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp cold milk

## Instructions
1. Roast pumpkin and mushrooms at 200°C for 20 minutes, drizzle with oil.  
2. Prepare pastry by blending flour, parmesan, and butter. Add egg and milk, form dough, refrigerate 30 minutes.  
3. Roll out dough and line a tart tin.  
4. Arrange fillings inside, whisk eggs and pour over.  
5. Bake covered for 1 hour 30 minutes at 180°C, uncover and bake until golden.
