## Ingredients
- 800–900 g tea biscuits, roughly crushed  
- 1 cup hazelnuts, toasted and chopped  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder  
- 1 cup water  
- 150 g butter  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

### Ganache
- 1/2 cup cream  
- 120 g bittersweet chocolate

## Instructions
1. In a saucepan, heat sugar, cocoa, and water until smooth; add butter and vanilla; cook until melted.  
2. Pour syrup over crushed biscuits and nuts; mix.  
3. Press into a lined 20–22 cm springform; chill 3–4 hours or overnight.  
4. Heat cream; pour over chocolate; stir smooth. Spread over cold cake, decorate with extra nuts.
