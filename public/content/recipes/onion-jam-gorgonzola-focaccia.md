## Ingredients
- 500 g white bread flour  
- 420 g warm water  
- 12 g instant yeast  
- 1 tsp honey  
- 1 tbsp olive oil, plus more for pan and topping  
- 2 tsp sea salt  
- 1 large white onion jam (store-bought or homemade)  
- 200 g Gorgonzola or other blue cheese

## Instructions
1. Mix water, yeast, honey, oil, and salt; stir in flour until a sticky dough forms. Rest 10 minutes.  
2. Stretch-and-fold twice at 10-minute intervals; cover and cold-ferment 24–48 hours.  
3. Oil a 22×33 cm pan; gently tip in dough and dimple. Rest 1–2 hours.  
4. Heat oven to 220ºC. Dimple again; top with onion jam and chunks of Gorgonzola; drizzle olive oil.  
5. Bake 20–25 minutes until puffed and golden. Cool slightly, slice, and serve.
