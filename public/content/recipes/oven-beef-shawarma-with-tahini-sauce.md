## Ingredients

### For the Shawarma
- 1 kg beef cuts (steak cuts)
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tsp shawarma spice
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- Tomato slices (optional)

### For the Tahini Sauce
- 1/2 cup tahini paste
- 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1/4 cup water
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- Sea salt (to taste)

## Instructions

### Shawarma
1. Cut beef into long strips about 10 cm long and 2 cm thick.
2. Rub with shawarma spice, vinegar, and olive oil. Cover and marinate overnight.
3. Place in baking dish with onion and garlic. Cover tightly with foil.
4. Bake at 200Â°C for 40 minutes.
5. Remove foil, add tomato slices, and bake 10 more minutes.

### Tahini Sauce
1. Whisk tahini, garlic, lemon juice, water, and salt until smooth.

Serve in pita or saj with tahini sauce and parsley.
