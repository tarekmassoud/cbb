## Ingredients
- Mixed vegetables (e.g., zucchini, broccoli, cauliflower, peppers, cherry tomatoes, onion)  
- Extra virgin olive oil  
- Dried oregano  
- Sea salt flakes  
- Cracked black pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 180°C. Line a tray with parchment.  
2. Toss vegetables with olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.  
3. Roast 35 minutes, tossing once, until tender and lightly charred. Serve hot.
