## Ingredients
- 1/3 cup (80 ml) water
- 2 tbsp gelatin powder
- 3 1/2 cups single/pouring cream
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla paste or extract
- Fresh figs, thinly sliced
- Warm honey, for drizzling

## Instructions
- Sprinkle gelatin over water; let sit 5 minutes.
- Heat cream, sugar, and vanilla over medium heat until sugar dissolves; bring just to a boil.
- Add gelatin mixture; stir to dissolve. Remove from heat.
- Divide into ramekins; chill 3â€“4 hours until set.
- Unmold, top with fig slices, and drizzle with honey.
