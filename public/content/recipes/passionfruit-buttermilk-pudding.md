## Ingredients
### Syrup
- 55g sugar  
- 80ml water  
- 80ml passionfruit pulp  

### Pudding
- 2.5 tsp powdered gelatine  
- 250ml buttermilk  
- 1 cup single cream  
- 1/2 cup white sugar  

## Instructions
1. Make syrup by heating sugar and water; add passionfruit.  
2. Soften gelatine in 2 tbsp buttermilk.  
3. Heat remaining buttermilk, cream, sugar.  
4. Add gelatine mixture.  
5. Refrigerate 5 hours.  
6. Serve with syrup.
