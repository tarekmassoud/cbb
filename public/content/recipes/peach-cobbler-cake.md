## Ingredients

### Peach Filling
- 2 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tbsp cornstarch  
- 454g peaches, peeled and sliced  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- Pinch of salt  

### Cake Batter
- 2 eggs  
- 3/4 cup sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 180g flour  
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1 cup milk  
- 43g melted butter  
- Sugar and cinnamon for topping  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 175°C and grease a 22cm pan.  
2. Mix filling ingredients and spread at bottom.  
3. Whisk batter ingredients; pour over peaches.  
4. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and bake 60 min.  
5. Cool 10 min before serving.
