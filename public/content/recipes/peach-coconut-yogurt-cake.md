## Ingredients
- 2 eggs  
- 3/4 cup oil  
- 1 cup yogurt  
- 1 tbsp lemon rind  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 1 cup coconut  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 260g flour  
- 6 peaches, diced  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  
2. Mix eggs, oil, yogurt, lemon, and sugar.  
3. Add dry ingredients and fold in peaches.  
4. Pour into pan and bake 45 min until golden.  
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve.
