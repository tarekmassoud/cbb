## Ingredients
- 450g flour  
- 75g brown sugar  
- 200g butter  
- 10 peaches  
- Almond meal  
- Honey  

## Instructions
1. Prepare pastry and chill.  
2. Mix peaches with sugar and honey.  
3. Roll pastry into strips and spiral over filling.  
4. Bake at 180°C for 45–50 minutes.
