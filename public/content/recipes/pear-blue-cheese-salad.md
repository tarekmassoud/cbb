## Ingredients

- Toasted sourdough bread  
- Rocket leaves  
- 200 g blue cheese (Roquefort or similar)  
- 2 pears, thinly sliced  
- 2 fennel bulbs, finely sliced  
- Freshly ground black pepper  
- 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar  
- 1/2 cup olive oil  
- A pinch of salt  

## Instructions

1. Toast or grill the sourdough slices until golden and set aside.
2. Wash and dry the rocket leaves and place them in a large serving bowl.
3. Thinly slice the pears and fennel.
4. Crumble the blue cheese over the salad.
5. Add the pears and fennel to the bowl.
6. Whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
7. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
8. Serve immediately with toasted sourdough on the side.
