## Ingredients
### Tangzhong
- ¾ cup milk
- 3½ tbsp flour

### Dough
- ½ cup milk, cold
- 450g all-purpose flour
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 25g butter, softened
- 7g instant yeast
- 1 tsp salt

### Filling
- 4–5 tbsp pesto
- 200g mozzarella or gruyère, grated

## Instructions
- In a small saucepan, whisk tangzhong flour and milk over medium heat, stirring until thickened; cool slightly.
- In a mixer bowl, combine dough ingredients with tangzhong; knead 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
- Shape into a ball, cover, and proof 60–90 minutes until doubled.
- Roll dough into a rectangle, spread with pesto, sprinkle cheese, then roll up and slice.
- Arrange in a lined pan, proof 30 minutes, then bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes until golden.
