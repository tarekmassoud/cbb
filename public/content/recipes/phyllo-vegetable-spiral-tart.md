## Ingredients
- 100g sweet potato, sliced  
- 2 portobello mushrooms, sliced  
- 1 small carrot, sliced  
- 1 small zucchini, sliced  
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced  
- 1 stick leek, sliced  
- 1 package phyllo dough  
- 4 tbsp olive oil

### Egg mixture
- 3 eggs  
- 200 ml milk  
- 1 tbsp sweet paprika powder  
- 1 tsp rosemary  
- ¼ tsp cinnamon powder  
- Salt

### Finish
- 3 stalks parsley, chopped  
- 1 garlic clove, pressed  
- 4 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions
1. Arrange phyllo in a spiral in a tart pan; brush with olive oil. Bake 30 minutes at 180°C until golden.  
2. Whisk eggs, milk, paprika, rosemary, cinnamon, and salt.  
3. Scatter prepared vegetables over the phyllo; pour over egg mixture.  
4. Bake another 30 minutes until set.  
5. Mix parsley, garlic, and olive oil; brush over tart before serving.
