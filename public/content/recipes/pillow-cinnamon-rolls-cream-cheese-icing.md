## Ingredients
### Tangzhong
- 3/4 cup milk  
- 2 tbsp flour

### Dough
- 1 cup warm milk  
- 450 g all-purpose flour  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 3 tbsp sugar  
- 3 tsp instant yeast  
- 85 g unsalted butter, softened  
- 1 egg

### Filling
- 120 g butter, melted  
- 160 g light brown sugar  
- 3 tbsp flour  
- 1 tbsp cinnamon  
- Pinch of salt

### Icing
- 170 g cream cheese, soft  
- 40 g butter, soft  
- 170 g icing sugar  
- 2 tbsp cream

## Instructions
1. Cook tangzhong over medium heat until thick; cool.  
2. Mix dough ingredients with tangzhong to a soft dough; knead smooth. Rise until doubled.  
3. Roll into a 30×40 cm rectangle. Spread with butter; mix brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, and salt and scatter on top.  
4. Roll up tightly; slice 12 rolls. Arrange in a greased dish, cover, and rise 30–40 minutes.  
5. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes until golden.  
6. Beat icing ingredients smooth; spread over warm rolls.
