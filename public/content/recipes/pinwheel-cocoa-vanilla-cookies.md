## Ingredients
- 200g unsalted butter, at room temperature
- ¾ cup sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 egg
- 300g plain flour
- 30g cocoa powder

## Instructions
- In a mixer, cream butter and sugar. Beat in vanilla, then egg. Mix in flour to form a soft dough.
- Divide dough in half; knead cocoa into one half.
- Wrap each dough and chill 30 minutes.
- Roll both doughs into sheets. Stack, then roll up tightly lengthwise to form a log.
- Chill until firm, slice into discs, and place on a lined tray.
- Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes or until just golden.
