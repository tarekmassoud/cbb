## Ingredients
### Cake
- 3 eggs  
- 190g sugar  
- ½ cup (125g) yogurt  
- ½ cup (125 ml) vegetable oil  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  
- 3 cups (360g) almond meal  
- 1 cup (150g) all-purpose flour  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 3 large plums, wedged

### Vanilla syrup
- 1 cup water  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 1 vanilla bean, split and seeds scraped

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a 24 cm springform tin.  
2. Whisk eggs, sugar, yogurt, oil, and vanilla.  
3. Sift flour and baking powder; add with almond meal and mix to combine.  
4. Pour into tin and top with plum wedges. Bake 30–35 minutes or until a skewer tests clean.  
5. For syrup, simmer water, sugar, and vanilla 6–8 minutes.  
6. Pour half the hot syrup over the warm cake. Serve warm or cooled with remaining syrup.
