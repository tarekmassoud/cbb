## Ingredients

- 3 eggs  
- 190 g sugar  
- 1/2 cup (125 g) yogurt  
- 1/2 cup (125 ml) vegetable oil  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  
- 3 cups (360 g) almond meal  
- 1 cup (150 g) all-purpose flour  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 3 large plums, sliced

**Vanilla Syrup**
- 1 cup water  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 1 vanilla bean, split and seeds scraped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F). Line a 24 cm round springform tin with baking paper.  
2. Whisk eggs, sugar, yogurt, oil, and vanilla.  
3. Sift flour and baking powder; stir in. Add almond meal and combine.  
4. Pour into tin, top with plum wedges, and bake 30–35 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean.  
5. Meanwhile, make the syrup: simmer water, sugar, and vanilla (bean + seeds) 6–8 minutes until slightly thickened.  
6. Pour half the hot syrup over the warm cake; serve warm or cooled with extra syrup.
