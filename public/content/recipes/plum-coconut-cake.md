## Ingredients
- 2 eggs  
- ⅔ cup vegetable oil  
- 1 cup Greek yogurt  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- Zest of 1 lemon  
- 1 cup desiccated coconut  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 260g flour  
- 6 plums, sliced  

## Instructions
1. Whisk eggs, oil, yogurt, sugar, and zest.  
2. Add dry ingredients and mix gently.  
3. Pour into lined tin, top with plums and sugar.  
4. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes.
