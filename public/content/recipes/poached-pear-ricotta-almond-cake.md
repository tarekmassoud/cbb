## Ingredients

### Poached Pears
- 6 small firm pears, peeled  
- 1 cup (220 g) sugar  
- 2 cinnamon sticks  
- 1 liter water

### Cake
- 4 eggs  
- 3/4 cup (165 g) sugar  
- 300 g fresh ricotta  
- 1 1/4 cups (180 g) almond meal  
- 1/4 cup flour  
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder  
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon  
- 2 tsp vanilla extract  
- Icing sugar, to finish

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 160°C. Poach pears with sugar, cinnamon, and water 10 minutes until just tender. Cool slightly.  
2. Whisk eggs and sugar until pale. Fold in ricotta, almond meal, flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and vanilla.  
3. Pour into a 24 cm springform lined with baking paper. Arrange pears over the batter; press slightly in.  
4. Bake 1 hour–1 hour 10 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean. Cool; transfer to a plate and dust with icing sugar.
