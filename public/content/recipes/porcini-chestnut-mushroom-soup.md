## Ingredients
- 10 g dried porcini
- 500 g chestnut mushrooms
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves
- 600 ml chicken stock
- 100 ml cream
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Soak porcini in boiling water.
2. Sauté onion and garlic in butter and olive oil.
3. Add mushrooms and cook until golden.
4. Add porcini and stock. Simmer 15 minutes.
5. Blend until smooth, stir in cream and adjust seasoning.
