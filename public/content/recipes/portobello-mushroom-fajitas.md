## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 clove garlic, crushed  
- 1 tsp ground cumin  
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika  
- 500 g portobello mushrooms, sliced  
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes  
- 1 cup sweet corn  
- 2 avocados  
- 1 lime, juiced  
- 1 bunch chopped coriander  
- 1 small chili, finely chopped  
- 8 small tortilla wraps  
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions
1. Fry garlic in oil; add mushrooms, tomatoes, corn, and spices. Cook until softened.  
2. Mash avocados with lime juice, chili, coriander, salt, and pepper.  
3. Warm tortillas, fill with mixture, and top with guacamole. Serve immediately.
