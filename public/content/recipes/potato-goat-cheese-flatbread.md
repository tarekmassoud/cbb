## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast  
- 1 1/4 cups warm water  
- 3 3/4 cups flour  
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt  
- 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil  
- 5 small potatoes, thinly sliced  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 200g goat cheese  
- Parmesan  
- Red pepper flakes  
- Truffle oil  

## Instructions
1. Activate yeast in warm water.  
2. Mix dough; knead until smooth.  
3. Rise 45 minutes. Punch down.  
4. Roll dough into a rectangle.  
5. Top with potatoes, oil, salt, pepper.  
6. Bake until crisp.  
7. Add goat cheese, Parmesan, and truffle oil.
