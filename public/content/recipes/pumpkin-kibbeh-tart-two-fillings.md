## Ingredients

### Kibbeh Mixture
- 2 cups fine bulgur  
- 1 medium onion, grated  
- 3½ cups pumpkin purée (or ~2 kg roasted pumpkin, drained well)  
- 2 tbsp flour  
- 1 tsp salt  
- ¼ tsp black pepper

### Mom’s Filling
- 1 large onion, chopped  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight and peeled)  
- 1 generous bunch spinach leaves, roughly chopped  
- 1 tsp sumac  
- Salt to taste  
- A squeeze of fresh lemon juice

### Tante Laure’s Filling
- 1 large onion, cut into wedges  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- ½ cup (or more) walnuts, crushed  
- ½ cup sultanas (golden raisins)  
- 1 tsp sumac  
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

### Kibbeh Base
1. Mix all kibbeh ingredients together until well combined. Let rest 30 minutes.  
2. Divide and press the mixture into two 20 cm baking pans.  
3. Bake at 180°C for 35–40 minutes.

### Mom’s Filling
1. Sauté the onion in olive oil, then add chickpeas and spinach.  
2. Season with sumac and salt. Finish with a splash of lemon juice.

### Tante Laure’s Filling
1. Sauté the onion in olive oil, then add walnuts and raisins.  
2. Season with sumac and salt.  
3. Spoon desired filling into each tart and serve warm.
