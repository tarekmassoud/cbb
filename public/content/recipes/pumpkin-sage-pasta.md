## Ingredients

- 225 g pasta (fusilli col buco or similar)  
- 2 Tbsp olive oil  
- 1 large shallot, diced (about 1/2 cup)  
- 4–6 garlic cloves, roughly chopped  
- 10 fresh sage leaves  
- 400 g canned pumpkin purée + 400 ml coconut milk (or cream)  
- 1/2 tsp sea salt, to taste  
- 1/4 tsp black pepper  
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg  
- 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon  
- Grated Parmesan or pecorino  
- Lemon zest (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente; reserve some cooking water.  
2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sage and sauté until crisp; set aside.  
3. Add shallot and sauté ~5 minutes until soft, then add garlic.  
4. Stir in pumpkin purée and coconut milk. Simmer, season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Adjust with pasta water if needed.  
5. Toss pasta with the sauce. Finish with Parmesan, lemon zest, and the crispy sage leaves. Serve immediately.
