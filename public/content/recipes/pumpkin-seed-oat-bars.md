## Ingredients
- 2 cups oats (160 g)
- 1 cup mixed nuts (100 g; almonds and cashews)
- 4 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of sea salt
- 1/4 cup maple syrup (60 ml)
- 1/4 cup coconut oil (60 ml)
- 1/2 cup peanut or almond butter (120 g)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. On a lined tray, toast oats, nuts, and pumpkin seeds for 10 minutes.
2. Mix peanut butter, maple syrup, coconut oil, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl until smooth.
3. Stir in the toasted dry mix until fully coated.
4. Press firmly into a lined 20 cm square tin. Bake 20–25 minutes at 120°C.
5. Cool completely, then cut into bars. Store airtight.
