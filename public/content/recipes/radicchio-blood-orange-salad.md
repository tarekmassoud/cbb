## Ingredients
- 2 red chicory bulbs  
- 2 fennel bulbs  
- 1 radicchio  
- 3–5 asparagus spears  
- 3 blood oranges  
- 30g pistachios  
- 4 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 tbsp honey mustard  
- 3 tsp lemon juice  
- 1 tsp white wine vinegar  

## Instructions
1. Slice chicory, fennel, and radicchio.  
2. Add asparagus, orange slices, and pistachios.  
3. Whisk dressing ingredients together and drizzle over.  
4. Toss lightly and serve chilled.
