## Ingredients

### Salad
- 1 rainbow beetroot, thinly sliced  
- 1 cup red cabbage, shredded  
- 4 carrots, peeled and julienned  
- 2 avocados, cut into wedges  
- 1/3 cup sunflower and pumpkin seeds, toasted  
- 1/3 cup mung beans  
- 2 cups sunflower sprouts

### Tahini Dressing
- 400 g chickpeas, cooked and drained  
- 2 tbsp tahini  
- 1 tbsp lemon rind  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice  
- 2 tsp ground coriander  
- 1/2 cup water  
- Sea salt and cracked black pepper

## Instructions
1. Blend dressing ingredients until smooth.  
2. Spread over a large plate; top with sliced vegetables, beans, and seeds.  
3. Sprinkle with extra seeds and serve.
