## Ingredients
### Dough
- 7 g active dry yeast  
- 2 tbsp caster sugar  
- 230 ml lukewarm water  
- 180 ml warm milk  
- Zest of 1 orange  
- 480 g flour  
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon  
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil  
- Pinch of salt

### Raspberry filling
- 200 g frozen raspberries  
- 2/3 cup sugar

## Instructions
1. Bloom yeast with sugar in water. Stir in warm milk and orange zest.  
2. Mix flour, cinnamon, oil, and salt; add yeast mixture and knead 5–6 minutes until smooth and elastic.  
3. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and rise until doubled.  
4. For the filling, cook raspberries with sugar until thickened; cool.  
5. Roll dough to a 25×55 cm rectangle. Spread filling in a stripe; roll up from the long side.  
6. Slice roll lengthwise, twist the two strands, and shape into a wreath on a lined tray.  
7. Bake at 200°C for 20–30 minutes until golden. Cool before slicing.
