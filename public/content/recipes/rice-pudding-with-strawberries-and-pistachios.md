## Ingredients
- 1½ cups (280g) cooked white rice, chilled  
- 1 cup (250ml) single (pouring) cream  
- 1 cup (125ml) milk  
- 1/4 cup (90g) honey  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 250g strawberries, hulled and halved  
- 1 tbsp honey (for the strawberries)  
- 1 tsp rosewater  
- Pistachios, soaked and peeled

## Instructions
1. In a medium saucepan, combine rice, cream, milk, 1/4 cup honey, and vanilla. Heat over medium, stirring for 6–8 minutes until hot and thickened.  
2. Divide into 4 serving glasses and refrigerate 1 hour or until chilled.  
3. Toss strawberries with 1 tbsp honey and rosewater.  
4. Top each glass with the strawberry mixture and pistachios. Serve.
