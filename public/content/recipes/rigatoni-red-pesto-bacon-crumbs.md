## Ingredients
- 1 pack rigatoni
- 100 g grated Parmesan, divided
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 200 g bacon, cut into bits
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp capers
- Salt and black pepper

Red pesto:
- 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained (reserve oil)
- 3 garlic cloves, chopped
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2/3 cup basil leaves
- 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
- 50 g grated Parmesan
- 1/4 cup reserved sun-dried tomato oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook rigatoni in salted boiling water until al dente; drain.
2. Blend pesto ingredients to a slightly textured paste, adding a splash of water if needed.
3. For crumbs, heat 2 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and capers; toast. Season with chili flakes, salt, and pepper; set aside.
4. In the same skillet, cook bacon in 2 tbsp oil until crisp.
5. Toss pasta with pesto, half the bacon, Parmesan, and some crumbs. Garnish with extra chili flakes and black pepper. Serve warm.
