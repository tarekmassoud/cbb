## Ingredients

### For the Roast Beef

- 1 1/2 kg topside of beef
- 1 medium onion halved
- 1 carrot
- 2 celery sticks
- olive oil
- sea salt and black pepper
- 10 garlic gloves
- 2 cups of water
- 3 cups of dry white wine

### For the Beans

- a medium bag of frozen beans
- 8-10 large crushed garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup of olive oil
- sea salt and black pepper

### For the Gravy

- 2 tbsp of flour
- 2 tbsp of butter
- juices from meat
- 2 tsp of dijon mustard

## Instructions

### Roast Beef

1. Stick the meat with a knife and incorporate a glove of garlic inside each hall
2. Drizzle the meat with olive oil sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Heat a pressure cooker over high heat brown roast on all sides add the onion carrot and celery sticks and brown with meat
4. Pour the wine all over the meat,add the water,seal the lid and cook for around 1 and a half hours on medium heat
5. Open the cooker when cooled remove the meat and refrigerate for half an hour(this will help cutting it into fine pieces)

### Gravy

1. Pour the remaining vegetables and the meat juices into a stainer and pressing the vegetables with a spoon. Set aside
2. Melt the better in a saucepan and add the flour stir constantly until its golden caramel color
3. Whisk in the meat liquid and stir constantly
4. Bring it to the simmer and let it bubble until thicken for about 5 minutes
5. Add the mustard and stir

### Green Beans

1. Wash the beans until smooth, drain well
2. poor olive oil in a large skillet with a lid then add the crashed garlic over low heat until evenly distributed all over the pan
3. Add the beans whisk to combine cover for about 5 minutes stirring often
4. Uncover and cook for another 5 minutes over law heat season to taste with salt snd pepper
