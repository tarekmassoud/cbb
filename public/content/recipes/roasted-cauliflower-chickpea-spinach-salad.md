## Ingredients
- 1 cauliflower, cut into florets  
- 100 g chickpeas, cooked and drained  
- Olive oil, sea salt  
- 2 tsp ground cumin  
- 1 tsp ground turmeric  
- 1 tsp ground coriander  
- 6 medjool dates, stones removed, chopped  
- 100 g pine nuts, toasted  
- 1 tsp cumin seeds  
- 1 tsp fennel seeds  
- 1 tsp pumpkin seeds  
- Bunch of mint leaves, shredded  
- 3 tbsp olive oil  
- 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar  
- Pinch of sea salt

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C. Toss cauliflower florets with olive oil and sea salt on a baking tray; roast 20–25 minutes until golden. Add chickpeas for the last 5 minutes.  
2. In a pan, toast pine, cumin, fennel, and pumpkin seeds for 2–3 minutes; stir occasionally.  
3. Whisk olive oil, apple cider vinegar, and a pinch of sea salt.  
4. Combine roasted veg, dates, seeds, and mint. Dress and serve warm.
