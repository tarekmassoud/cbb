## Ingredients
- 4 pears  
- 100g almond cut lengthwise  
- 200g gorgonzola  
- Thyme sprigs  
- Honey  

## Instructions
1. Halve pears, scoop seeds.  
2. Arrange cut side up in baking tray.  
3. Fill with gorgonzola; top with almonds.  
4. Sprinkle thyme.  
5. Bake 20 minutes; drizzle honey.
