## Ingredients

### For the Bowl
- 1 small pumpkin, halved and seeded  
- Olive oil, thyme, salt, pepper  
- Honey or maple syrup (for drizzle)  
- Cooked wild rice, bulgur, or quinoa  
- Grilled salmon fillets  

### For the Gremolata
- A bunch of parsley  
- 2 cloves garlic, finely sliced  
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Pinch of salt  

## Instructions
1. Roast the pumpkin halves at 200°C with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.  
2. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup and roast another 10 minutes.  
3. Serve with grains and salmon, topping with gremolata.
