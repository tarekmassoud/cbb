## Ingredients
### Cauliflower
- 5 baby cauliflowers
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp garam masala
- Olive oil, salt, and black pepper
- Toasted hazelnuts, roughly chopped (for serving)

### Raita
- 300g Lebanese yogurt
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 small bunch coriander, finely chopped
- Olive oil
- 6 tbsp baby capers, rinsed and drained

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 200°C. Trim the cauliflowers so they sit flat if needed.
- Mix the spices with oil, salt, and pepper. Rub all over the cauliflowers and roast 30–35 minutes until tender and browned.
- For the raita, combine yogurt, onion, coriander, a drizzle of olive oil, and a pinch of salt.
- Heat a little oil in a pan and fry the capers until crisp.
- Serve the cauliflower with raita, fried capers, and chopped hazelnuts.
