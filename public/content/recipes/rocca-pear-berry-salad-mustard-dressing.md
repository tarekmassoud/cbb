## Ingredients

### Salad

- 2 cups fresh rocca (arugula)  
- 1 ripe pear, thinly sliced  
- ½ cup soft goat cheese, crumbled  
- ½ cup fresh blueberries and blackberries  
- ¼ cup walnuts  

### Mustard Dressing

- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard  
- 2 tbsp honey  
- ¼ cup olive oil  
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar  
- Salt and pepper, to taste  

## Instructions

1. In a large salad bowl, combine the rocca, sliced pear, soft cheese, berries, and walnuts.  
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper until emulsified.  
3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving and toss gently.  
4. Serve immediately.
