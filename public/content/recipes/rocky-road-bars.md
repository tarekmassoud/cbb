## Ingredients
- 300 g dark chocolate  
- 120 g butter  
- 90 g maple syrup  
- 100 g marshmallows  
- 200 g crushed biscuits  
- 50 g shredded coconut  
- 50 g toasted peeled hazelnuts  
- 50 g peanuts

## Instructions
1. Melt chocolate, butter, and maple syrup (bain-marie or microwave) until smooth.  
2. In a large bowl, add crushed biscuits, marshmallows, coconut, pistachios (if using), hazelnuts, and peanuts.  
3. Pour in melted chocolate; mix to coat.  
4. Press into a lined tray; chill 5â€“6 hours until set. Slice and enjoy.
