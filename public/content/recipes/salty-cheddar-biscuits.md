## Ingredients
- 50 g self-raising flour  
- 25 g soft butter  
- 80 g finely grated mature cheddar cheese  
- 20 g finely grated Parmesan  
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with non-stick baking parchment.  
2. Place all ingredients in a food processor and process until the dough comes together.  
3. Roll out the orange dough between two nylon sheets and cut out your shapes.  
4. Place on the baking tray and bake for 8–10 minutes or until puffy and golden. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
