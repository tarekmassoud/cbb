## Ingredients

- 2 cans sardines
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Chili flakes
- 1 little red onion, finely chopped
- Capers
- Fresh parsley
- Fresh cress
- Thin lemon slices, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a plate, add the oil from the 2 cans of sardines and the juice of 1/2 lemon.
2. Season with salt, black pepper, and chili flakes.
3. Add the sardines and mash them lightly with a fork.
4. Mix in capers, finely chopped red onion, a little fresh parsley, and a handful of fresh cress.
5. Give everything a good mix until combined.
6. Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with thin lemon slices.
7. Serve with your favorite crackers.
