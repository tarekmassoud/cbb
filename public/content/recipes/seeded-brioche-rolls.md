## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups warm water  
- 2 1/2 tsp dry yeast  
- 1 tsp + 1/4 cup sugar  
- 600 g all-purpose flour  
- 370 g whole wheat flour  
- 1 tbsp salt  
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds  
- 1/4 cup sunflower seeds  
- 1/4 cup flax seeds  
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds  
- 4 eggs  
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil  
- 1/4 cup molasses or honey  

### Topping
- 1 egg yolk  
- Mixed seeds for garnish

## Instructions
1. Mix water, yeast, and 1 tsp sugar; rest until foamy.  
2. Combine flours, salt, remaining sugar, and seeds.  
3. Add wet mix, eggs, oil, and molasses; knead smooth.  
4. Rise 2–3 hrs. Divide, shape into coils, rise 30 min.  
5. Brush with egg yolk, sprinkle seeds, and bake at 200ºC for 25 min.
