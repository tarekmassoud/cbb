## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp sesame oil  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 lb medium-large shrimp, cleaned  
- 1 cup frozen peas  
- 2 large eggs  
- 2 cups cooked rice (white or basmati)  
- 4–5 green onions, trimmed and sliced  
- ½ cup tamari sauce (or coconut aminos)  
- ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper  

## Instructions

1. Heat the sesame oil and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  
2. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and just cooked through. Remove and set aside.  
3. In the same skillet, push the oil to one side, crack in the eggs, and scramble until just set.  
4. Add the frozen peas and stir-fry for a minute.  
5. Return the shrimp to the pan, then add the cooked rice and green onions. Toss to combine.  
6. Pour in the tamari sauce and season with black pepper. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until everything is heated through.  
7. Serve hot, garnished with extra green onions if desired.
