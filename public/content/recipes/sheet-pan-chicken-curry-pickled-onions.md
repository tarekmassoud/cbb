## Ingredients

### Chicken & Cauliflower
- 3 garlic cloves, grated  
- 1 tbsp curry powder  
- 1 tsp sea salt  
- 1/2 tsp turmeric  
- 1 tsp garam masala  
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 450 g chicken breast, cubed  
- 1/2 head cauliflower, in florets  
- Fresh cilantro, to serve

### Pickled Onions
- 2 red onions, sliced  
- 3/4 cup water  
- 1 tbsp peppercorns  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 garlic clove, crushed  
- 1 star anise  
- 1/2 tsp coriander seeds  
- 1/4 cup cider vinegar

## Instructions
1. Combine pickling ingredients; boil, pour over onions, and cool.  
2. Mix chicken, cauliflower, and spices; spread on baking sheet.  
3. Roast 15–20 min at 200ºC.  
4. Serve with pickled onions and cilantro.
