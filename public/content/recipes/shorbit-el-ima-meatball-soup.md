## Ingredients
- 500g minced lamb  
- 140g basmati rice  
- 6 medium tomatoes, diced  
- 1 bunch parsley, chopped  
- 2 medium onions  
- 1L water  
- Salt and pepper  
- Juice of 1 lemon  

## Instructions
1. Mix lamb, parsley, 1 onion (finely chopped), 1 tsp salt, and pepper.  
2. Shape into small balls.  
3. Sauté meatballs in oil until browned.  
4. Add tomatoes, remaining onion, rice, and water.  
5. Simmer 20 minutes or until rice is cooked.  
6. Add lemon juice before serving.
