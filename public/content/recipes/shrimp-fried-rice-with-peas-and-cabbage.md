## Ingredients
- 600 g shrimp
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups cooked rice
- 150 g peas
- 2 carrots
- 2 cups cabbage
- Soy sauce
- Butter
- Sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Sear shrimp and remove.
2. Scramble eggs.

4. Add rice and butter.
5. Mix in shrimp, eggs, soy sauce, sesame oil.
6. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.
