## Ingredients

**For the Sauce**
- 1 tbsp scallions, sliced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp sesame oil
- 3/4 cup water
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1–2 tbsp chili garlic sauce (optional)

**For the Stir-Fry**
- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 tsp avocado oil (or other high-heat oil)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced large
- 1 yellow bell pepper, diced large
- 1 onion, diced large
- 1 zucchini, sliced into half-moons
- 1 tbsp white distilled vinegar
- 3 scallions, minced
- Cooked white basmati rice, for serving

## Method

1. Whisk the sauce ingredients; set aside.
2. Stir-fry shrimp in 2 tsp oil for 2–3 minutes per side; remove.
3. In the same pan, add remaining oil, then garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
4. Add peppers, onion, and zucchini; stir-fry 4–5 minutes.
5. Return shrimp, pour in sauce, and cook 1–2 minutes until thickened.
6. Off the heat, add vinegar and scallions. Serve with rice.
