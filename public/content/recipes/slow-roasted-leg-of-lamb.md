## Ingredients
- 2.5 kg bone-in leg of lamb
- 2 rosemary sprigs, chopped
- 1 lemon, quartered
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Sea salt and cracked black pepper
- 6 tbsp mayonnaise (optional for serving)
- 800 g sweet potato, chopped
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 cups green olives
- 1 cup water

## Instructions
- Preheat oven to 200°C. Rub lamb with oil, salt, and pepper; make small slits and tuck in rosemary and lemon.
- Place lamb in a deep roasting tray with water; cover with foil and roast 2 hours.
- Uncover; add sweet potato and cinnamon, drizzle honey. Lower to 180°C and roast 30 minutes.
- Add olives; roast 10 minutes more. Rest 10 minutes, then carve and serve.
