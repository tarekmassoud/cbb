## Ingredients

### For the Tangzhong

- 45ml of water
- 45ml of milk
- 15g flour

### For the Dough

- 300g flour
- 15g of milk powder
- 50g of sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp of instant baking yeast
- 115g of milk
- 1 large beaten egg
- 60g of melted then tempered butter

## Instructions

### Tangzhong

1. Pour milk,water and flour in a small saucepan heat over low heat stirring constantly until thickened
2. Set aside in a small bowl to cool

### Dough

1. Preheat oven to 180°C
2. Melt butter leave to cool
3. Mix flour,sugar,salt, milk and yeast
4. Make a well in the above mixture pour the beaten egg,Tangzhong,milk and Butter
5. Mix and knead for about 5 minutes
6. Form a ball dough cover with cling film and a tea towel. Let rise for about 1h 30m
7. Degas the dough on a floured surface cut and roll into 8 equal balls
8. Place the balls in a 23cm round thin covered with non stick baking sheet and let it rest until doubled in size for around 50min
9. Brush with beaten egg and bake for 25 minutes or until having a nice golden crust
