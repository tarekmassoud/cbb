## Ingredients
- 2 kg small potatoes, boiled 20 minutes in salty water  
- 7 cloves garlic  
- 3 sprigs fresh rosemary  
- Salt and pepper  
- ½ tsp flour  
- Olive oil

### Salad
- 1 large cucumber  
- 1 large green zucchini  
- 1 large yellow zucchini  
- 2 carrots  
- 1 white radish  
- Handful mint and basil leaves  
- ¼ cup vinegar  
- ¼ cup lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Bake potatoes with garlic for 15 minutes; remove and gently smash.  
2. Drizzle with olive oil; sprinkle flour, salt, pepper, and rosemary. Roast 30 minutes until crisp.  
3. Peel vegetables into ribbons; toss with herbs.  
4. Mix vinegar and lemon juice; dress the salad.  
5. Serve potatoes with the dressed salad on top.
