## Ingredients

- 320 g tagliatelle (turmeric-infused if available)  
- 400 g smoked salmon, cut into strips  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 small brick of cream  
- A bunch of fresh basil, chopped  
- Salt and black pepper  
- 1 tbsp pink berries  
- Grated Parmesan or pecorino, for garnish  

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the smoked salmon strips, chopped basil, and lemon juice. Mix well and keep in the fridge.  
2. Cook the tagliatelle in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions.  
3. In a pan, heat the cream over medium heat and season with salt and black pepper.  
4. Add the cooked tagliatelle to the pan with the cream sauce, then add the salmon mixture. Toss gently until the pasta is evenly coated.  
5. Serve topped with grated Parmesan or pecorino and pink berries. Enjoy immediately.
