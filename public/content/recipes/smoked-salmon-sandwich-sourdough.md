## Ingredients

- Sourdough bread
- Avocado
- Lemon juice
- Cottage cheese
- Dill
- Rocket leaves
- Smoked salmon
- Red onion
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Mash the avocado and mix with a squeeze of lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
2. Mix the cottage cheese with the dill.
3. Spread the bread with a generous layer of avocado. Top it with the cottage cheese.
4. Add rocket leaves, smoked salmon, and thinly sliced red onion. Serve.
