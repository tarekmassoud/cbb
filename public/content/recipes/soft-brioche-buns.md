## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp dry yeast  
- 1 cup lukewarm water  
- 4 eggs  
- 4 cups flour  
- 80g butter  
- 1 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tsp salt  

### Egg Wash
- 1 egg yolk  
- 1 tsp milk  
- Sesame or chia seeds  

## Instructions
1. Activate yeast with water and sugar until foamy.  
2. Mix flour, eggs, salt, and yeast. Knead 8 minutes.  
3. Add butter and knead until smooth.  
4. Cover and rise 3 hours in fridge.  
5. Shape buns, rest 30 minutes.  
6. Brush with egg wash, sprinkle seeds.  
7. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
