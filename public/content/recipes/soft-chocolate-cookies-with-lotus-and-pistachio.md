## Ingredients

- 200 g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
- 1 cup (175 g) light brown sugar
- 3/4 cup (165 g) white sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 extra egg yolk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp water
- 2 cups (300 g) all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup (35 g) cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp milk
- 300 g chopped dark chocolate
- Store-bought Lotus spread
- Store-bought pistachio spread

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line 2 baking trays with parchment paper.
2. In a bowl, beat the butter and both sugars on medium speed for 6 to 8 minutes until sandy in texture.
3. Add the egg, extra yolk, and vanilla, then beat on high speed for 2 minutes until pale and creamy.
4. In a small bowl, mix baking powder, baking soda, and water, then add to the mixture.
5. Add flour, salt, cocoa powder, milk, and chopped chocolate. Mix on low speed until just combined.
6. Scoop 1/4 cup portions of dough, roll into balls, and place on trays, leaving space between each.
7. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes or until the cookies are firm to the touch.
8. Let cool on the tray for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
9. Slightly warm the Lotus and pistachio spreads in the microwave for a few seconds until smooth and spreadable.
10. Spread over the cooled cookies.
