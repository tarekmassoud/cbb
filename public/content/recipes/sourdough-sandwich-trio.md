## Ingredients

### Tuna Sandwich (beetroot sourdough)
- Tuna (2 cans)  
- Celery (1/4 cup), spring onion (1/4 cup), parsley (1/4 cup), mayo (2 tbsp), Dijon mustard (1 tsp), lemon juice (1 lemon), salt and pepper  
- Lettuce, tomato, capers

### Burrata, Tomato & Garlic Confit (pumpkin or sweet potato sourdough)
- Cherry tomatoes, garlic confit, olive oil, pesto, burrata  
- Optional: roast garlic, place garlic and tomato covered with olive oil and cook at 200°C for 20 minutes

### Smoked Salmon Sandwich (sweet potato sourdough)
- Avocado, lemon juice, cottage cheese, dill, rocket leaves, smoked salmon, red onion

## Method
1. Tuna: Combine tuna with celery, spring onion, parsley, mayo, Dijon, lemon juice; season. Toast sourdough, add lettuce, sliced tomato, tuna mix, then capers.  
2. Burrata: Toast sourdough, spread pesto, top with roasted garlic, cherry tomatoes, and burrata. Drizzle with olive oil and fresh basil leaves.  
3. Salmon: Mash avocado with lemon, salt, and pepper. Spread on toast, add cottage cheese, dill, rocket, smoked salmon, and thin red onion.
