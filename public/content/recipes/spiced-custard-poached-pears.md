## Ingredients
### Custard
- 1L pouring cream  
- 4 eggs + 2 egg yolks  
- 3/4 cup white sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon  
- Freshly grated nutmeg  

### Poached Pears
- 165g white sugar  
- 190ml water  
- 65ml orange liqueur  
- 1 vanilla bean  
- 1 cinnamon stick  
- Peel of 1 orange  
- 10 baby pears  

## Instructions
1. Make caramel sauce: heat sugar and water until deep caramel.  
2. Remove from heat; add water, liqueur, vanilla, cinnamon, orange peel.  
3. Poach pears 10 minutes; set aside.  
4. Preheat oven to 160Â°C.  
5. Mix custard ingredients; pour into dish.  
6. Bake 1 hour.  
7. Add pears on top and serve.
