## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp mustard seeds  
- 2 tsp fennel seeds  
- 2 tsp cumin seeds  
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric  
- 1 tsp chili powder  
- 1 tsp sea salt flakes + cracked black pepper  
- 6 duck legs (or 4–6, as needed)  
- 5 cm piece fresh ginger, sliced  
- 1 bulb garlic, halved  
- 2 sprigs curry leaves (optional)  
- 500 ml chicken stock

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200ºC. Toast mustard, fennel, and cumin 1 minute; grind. Mix with turmeric, chili, salt, and pepper.  
2. Rub spice mix over duck to coat; place in a roasting pan with ginger, garlic, curry leaves, and stock. Cover tightly with foil.  
3. Bake 2 hours until tender, turning halfway.  
4. Increase oven to 220ºC; uncover and roast 15–20 minutes skin-side-up until browned and crisp.
