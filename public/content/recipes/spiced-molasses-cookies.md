## Ingredients
- 340 g butter, softened  
- ½ cup sugar  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 2 eggs  
- ½ cup molasses  
- 3½ cups flour  
- 2 tsp baking soda  
- 1 tbsp ground ginger  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- ½ tsp cloves  
- Pinch salt

## Instructions
1. Cream butter and sugars; add eggs and molasses.  
2. Combine dry ingredients, mix into dough.  
3. Shape balls, roll in sugar, flatten slightly.  
4. Bake at 180°C for 8–10 minutes.
