## Ingredients

### Dough
6 cups white spelt flour (or AP), 1 tsp salt, 3/4–2 cups water, 6 tbsp olive oil

### Filling
1 pound spinach, 2 medium onions, 2 tbsp sumac, 1/2 tbsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, 1/4 cup lemon juice, 1/4 cup olive oil

## Instructions
- Mix flour and salt, add oil and water to form a soft dough; rest 1 hour.
- Blanch spinach 2 minutes; drain and squeeze dry. Chop onions; sauté lightly.
- Combine spinach with onions, sumac, salt, lemon juice, and olive oil.
- Preheat oven to 200°C. Roll dough, cut 8–9 cm rounds, add 1 tbsp filling, pinch into triangles.
- Brush with oil; bake 15–20 minutes until golden.
