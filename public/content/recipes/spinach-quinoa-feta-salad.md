## Ingredients
- Baby spinach  
- Quinoa, cooked  
- Tomatoes  
- Feta cheese  
- Pomegranate seeds  
- Green onions  
- Bell pepper or cucumber  
- Avocado  

### Dressing
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard  
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Toss spinach, quinoa, and vegetables in a bowl.  
2. Whisk dressing ingredients and pour over.  
3. Mix gently and serve immediately.
