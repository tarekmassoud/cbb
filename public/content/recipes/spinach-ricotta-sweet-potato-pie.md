## Ingredients
- 50 g butter, melted  
- 4 sheets filo pastry  
- 300 g baby spinach, roughly chopped  
- 300 g sweet potato, peeled and grated (or roasted cubes)  
- 200 g ricotta  
- 1/2 cup chopped dill  
- 2 cups single cream  
- 4 eggs  
- 1 garlic clove, crushed  
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C. Butter a 20×30 cm tin. Layer filo, brushing each sheet with butter.  
2. Spread spinach, sweet potato, ricotta, and dill.  
3. Whisk eggs, garlic, cream, salt, and pepper; pour over.  
4. Bake 30–35 minutes until set and golden. Serve with lemon wedges and a green salad.
