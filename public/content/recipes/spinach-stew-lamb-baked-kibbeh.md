## Ingredients

- 1000 g spinach  
- 250 g lamb meat, cut into large cubes  
- 1 large onion, finely chopped  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 tbsp crushed garlic  
- A bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped  
- 50 g toasted pine nuts  
- ½ cup lemon juice  
- 12 kibbeh krass  

## Instructions

1. Clean the spinach thoroughly, wash well, drain, roughly chop, and set aside.  
2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot, then add the onion and cook until soft.  
3. Add the garlic and cilantro and cook until fragrant.  
4. Add the lamb cubes, cover with water, season with salt, and cook until the meat is tender.  
5. Meanwhile, drizzle the kibbeh with a little olive oil and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for about 15 minutes, until heated through and lightly crisp.  
6. Add the chopped spinach to the pot with the cooked meat and cook briefly until the spinach wilts.  
7. Adjust seasoning and stir in the lemon juice.  

### To Serve

1. Spoon the spinach and lamb stew into a large serving dish.  
2. Arrange the baked kibbeh on top and sprinkle with toasted pine nuts.  
3. Serve hot with fluffy white rice and lemon wedges on the side.
