## Ingredients
- 250g spinach tagliatelle  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed  
- 1 onion, finely chopped  
- 250g mushrooms  
- 1/2 cup dry white wine  
- 1 pack Boursin “ail et fines herbes”  
- 12.5 cl cooking cream  
- Salt & cracked black pepper  

## Instructions
1. Bring water to a boil and cook pasta with salt.  
2. Heat oil in a sauté pan; add garlic and onion 2–3 minutes.  
3. Add mushrooms and wine; cook 2 minutes.  
4. Add Boursin and cream; stir until melted.  
5. Drain pasta and mix into the sauce.  
6. Serve with parsley leaves.
