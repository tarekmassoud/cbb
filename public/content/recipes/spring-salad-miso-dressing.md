## Ingredients
### Salad
- A few handfuls of sucrine lettuce and endive
- 2 small Granny Smith apples, thinly sliced
- 2 ripe avocados, sliced
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- 1/4 cup toasted pumpkin seeds
- 1/4 cup toasted walnuts

### Miso Dressing
- 2 tbsp barley miso paste
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/4 cup rice vinegar
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Arrange greens on a platter. Layer apples, avocado, cranberries, walnuts, and pumpkin seeds.  
2. In a jar combine miso, honey, olive oil, lemon juice, ginger, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Season with salt and pepper and shake well.  
3. Drizzle dressing over the salad just before serving.
