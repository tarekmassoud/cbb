## Ingredients
- 12 artichoke bottoms
- 250 g mushrooms
- Mozzarella
- Breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- Béchamel sauce

## Instructions
1. Cook artichokes until tender.
2. Sauté mushrooms and herbs.
3. Fill artichokes and top with béchamel.
4. Bake until golden.
