## Ingredients
- 8 medium potatoes, peeled, halved, and cored
- 300 g ground meat (beef or lamb)
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup pine nuts
- 3 cups water
- 1 cup strained tomato
- 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Sauté onion in olive oil 2 minutes then add meat. Cook through. Stir in pine nuts and set aside.  
2. Brush potato halves with olive oil and brown in an air fryer or oven 10 minutes.  
3. Fill potatoes with the meat mixture and place over tomatoes in a baking dish.  
4. Whisk strained tomato with water, salt, and pepper and pour over.  
5. Bake 30 minutes until potatoes are fork-tender. Serve over rice.
