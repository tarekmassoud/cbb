## Ingredients
- 2 eggs  
- ½ cup vegetable oil  
- 1 cup natural Greek yogurt  
- 1 tbsp lemon rind  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 1 cup desiccated coconut  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 260g all-purpose flour  
- Mixed summer fruits, thinly sliced  
- Extra sugar (optional)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a 20 x 30 cm tray.  
2. Whisk eggs, oil, yogurt, lemon rind, and sugar.  
3. Fold in coconut, baking powder, and flour.  
4. Spread into tray, top with fruits, and sprinkle extra sugar if desired.  
5. Bake 45 minutes or until a skewer tests clean.
