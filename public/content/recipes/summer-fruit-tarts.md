## Ingredients

### Pastry
- 2 cups flour  
- 3 tbsp sugar  
- 150g butter  
- 2–3 tbsp iced water  

### Pastry Cream
- 2 cups milk  
- 4 egg yolks  
- ⅓ cup sugar  
- ⅓ cup cornflour  
- 1 tsp vanilla  

## Instructions
1. Make pastry, chill, roll, and blind bake.  
2. Cook pastry cream until thick, cool completely.  
3. Fill shells with cream and top with fruit.
