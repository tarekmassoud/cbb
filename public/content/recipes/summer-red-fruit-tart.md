## Ingredients
- Pastry: 335 g flour, 80 g sugar, 150 g cold butter, 3 tsp cider vinegar, 90 ml iced water, 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
- Filling: 1/2 cup almond meal, 1.2 kg mixed stone fruits (wedges), 150 g raspberries, 1/4 cup caster sugar + extra for sprinkling, 1 egg yolk (for brushing), 1 tsp vanilla ice cream to serve (optional)

## Instructions
- Pulse flour, sugar, and butter to coarse crumbs. Add vinegar, water, and vanilla; pulse to a rough dough. Form a disc and chill 1 hour.
- Preheat oven to 200°C. Roll to a 34 cm round (3 mm thick) on baking paper.
- Place on a tray; spread almond meal. Toss fruit with sugar and vanilla; pile over almond layer. Fold edges over fruit.
- Brush with egg yolk; sprinkle sugar. Bake 30–35 minutes until pastry is golden and fruit blistered.
- Cool 10 minutes and serve.
