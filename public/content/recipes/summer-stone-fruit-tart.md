## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 335g flour  
- 80g sugar  
- 150g butter  
- 3 tbsp vinegar  
- 90ml iced water  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  

### For the Filling
- 1/2 cup almond meal  
- 1kg mixed stone fruits (peaches, apricots, plums)  
- 150g raspberries  
- 1/4 cup sugar  
- 1 egg yolk  

## Instructions
1. Make dough and chill 1 hour.  
2. Roll out, spread almond meal.  
3. Arrange fruits, fold edges, brush with egg, sprinkle sugar.  
4. Bake 30 min at 200°C.  
5. Serve with ice cream.
