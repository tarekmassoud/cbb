## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil  
- 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes (drained)  
- 120 g roasted red capsicum  
- 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts  
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan  
- 1 garlic clove  
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes  
- 1/2 tsp black pepper, plus to serve  
- 300 g fettuccine  
- 1 cup ricotta (to serve)  
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Blend oil, tomatoes, capsicum, pine nuts, parmesan, garlic, chili, and pepper until smooth.  
2. Boil pasta until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup cooking water and drain.  
3. Toss pasta with sauce, loosening with water as needed.  
4. Top with ricotta and extra pepper; serve immediately.
