## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil  
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced  
- Zest of 1 lemon  
- 200 g kale, stems removed, leaves shredded  
- 150 g rainbow chard leaves, shredded  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- Sea salt & cracked black pepper, to taste  
- 6 eggs  
- 1 long red chili, thinly sliced  
- 1/2 tsp sumac  
- Lemon wedges, to serve

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 180°C (350°F).  
2. Warm olive oil in an ovenproof pan over medium heat. Add onion, oil, and lemon zest. Cook ~6 minutes, stirring, until onion softens.  
3. Stir in kale; cook 2 minutes. Add chard; cook 1 minute.  
4. Pour in lemon juice; season with salt and pepper. Mix to combine.  
5. Make 6 small wells and crack in the eggs.  
6. Transfer pan to oven and bake 4–5 minutes, or until whites are just set.  
7. Remove, top with sliced chili and a sprinkle of sumac. Serve with lemon wedges.
