## Ingredients

- 15 to 18 soft Medjool dates, pitted
- 1/2 cup crushed walnuts
- 3 tbsp peanut butter
- 2 bananas
- 1/4 cup shredded coconut

## Instructions

1. Arrange the pitted dates side by side between two sheets of baking paper.
2. Using a rolling pin, roll them into one flat rectangle.
3. Sprinkle the crushed walnuts over the dates, then gently flip the date layer so the walnuts are on the outside.
4. Spread the peanut butter evenly over the surface.
5. Place the bananas horizontally in the center and sprinkle with shredded coconut.
6. Roll tightly like sushi, using the baking paper to help shape it.
7. Wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm.
8. Slice into rounds and enjoy cold.
