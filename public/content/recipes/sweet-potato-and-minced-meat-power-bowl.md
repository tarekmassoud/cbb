## Ingredients

### For the Sweet Potatoes

- 2 sweet potatoes
- A drizzle of olive oil
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Mozzarella cheese
- Cooked minced meat
- 1 avocado, sliced or diced

### For the Salad

- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1 small red onion, finely diced
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

### Sweet Potatoes

1. Slice the sweet potatoes in half and place them on a baking tray.
2. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.
3. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15 minutes.
4. Flip the sweet potatoes and bake for another 15 minutes, or until tender.
5. Remove from the oven and gently squash them with a fork.
6. While still hot, top with mozzarella cheese and let it melt.
7. Add the cooked minced meat on top.

### Salad

1. In a bowl, mix the cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt to make the salad.

### Serve

1. Add avocado and a generous spoonful of the salad over the sweet potatoes.
2. Serve immediately and enjoy.
