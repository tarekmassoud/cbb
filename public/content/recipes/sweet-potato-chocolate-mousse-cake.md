## Ingredients

- 6 small to medium sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 400 g dark chocolate (70%)
- 1 tsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 200 g additional dark chocolate
- 1/2 tsp coconut oil (for topping)
- Coconut flakes, optional

## Instructions

1. Peel and cut the sweet potatoes into chunks.
2. Boil them until very soft and tender. Drain well.
3. Using an immersion blender, mash and blend the sweet potatoes until smooth and creamy.
4. Melt the 400 g dark chocolate.
5. Add the cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and melted chocolate to the sweet potatoes.
6. Mix everything together until smooth and fully combined.
7. Transfer the mixture into a cake mold or serving dish and smooth the top.
8. Melt the additional 200 g dark chocolate with the coconut oil and pour it over the cake as a chocolate topping.
9. Sprinkle with coconut flakes if desired.
10. Refrigerate overnight until firm and chilled.
