## Ingredients
- 750 g sweet potato, peeled and cubed  
- 1 medium kohlrabi or celery root, cubed  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Sea salt and black pepper  
- 90 g chorizo, sliced  
- 3–4 eggs  
- 150 g parmesan or other hard cheese, grated

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 200°C. Toss sweet potato and kohlrabi with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a lined tray. Roast 25 minutes until tender and golden.  
2. Transfer to an oven-safe skillet. Add chorizo and toss over medium heat for 2–3 minutes.  
3. Make small wells, crack in the eggs, and sprinkle cheese over.  
4. Grill 5–6 minutes until eggs are just set and cheese is melted. Serve hot.
