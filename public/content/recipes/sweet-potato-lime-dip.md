## Ingredients
- 500g sweet potatoes  
- 4 cloves garlic  
- Olive oil  
- Lime juice  
- Kaffir lime leaves  
- Sunflower seeds  

## Instructions
1. Roast sweet potatoes with garlic and oil until soft.  
2. Blend with lime juice and seasoning.  
3. Top with toasted seeds and serve.
