## Ingredients

- 1 cup tahini
- 1 cup carob molasses
- 1 cup toasted sesame seeds
- Walnuts
- Almonds

## Instructions

1. Mix the tahini, carob molasses, and toasted sesame seeds until well combined.
2. Spread the mixture onto a tray and smooth the top.
3. Add a layer of walnuts, followed by a layer of almonds, gently pressing them into the mixture.
4. Freeze for 2 hours.
5. Slice and enjoy.
