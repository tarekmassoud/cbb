## Ingredients
- 3 large taro roots  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 2 medium brown onions, chopped  
- 1 cup (192 g) small brown lentils  
- Juice of 1 large lemon  
- 5 garlic cloves, crushed  
- Salt to your taste

## Instructions
1. Start by cooking the onion in olive oil until tender and fragrant.  
2. Add the peeled and chopped taro and mix.  
3. Add the lentils. Cover with water and bring to a boil until the first bubble appears.  
4. Cover and cook on medium heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the taro is well done and lentils are softened.  
5. Add the garlic and lemon juice, and let them all simmer together for another 5–10 minutes on low heat.  
6. Turn off the heat, taste, and adjust seasoning.
