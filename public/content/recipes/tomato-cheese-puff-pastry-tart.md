## Ingredients
- 1 puff pastry sheet  
- 3 tbsp Dijon mustard  
- 200g fondel cheese  
- A basket of mixed tomatoes  

## Instructions
1. Spread mustard on the pastry.  
2. Add cheese.  
3. Top with tomatoes.  
4. Drizzle olive oil, salt, pepper.  
5. Bake until golden and puffed.
