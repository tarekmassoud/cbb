## Ingredients
- 800g cherry tomatoes  
- 2 fennel bulbs  
- 1 bulb garlic  
- Rosemary sprigs  
- Salt + pepper  
- Olive oil  
- Labneh pearls  

## Instructions
1. Peel garlic; quarter fennel.  
2. Add tomatoes and garlic to tray; cover with olive oil.  
3. Add fennel + rosemary; season.  
4. Cook 1 hour until blistered.  
5. Serve with labneh pearls and baguette.
