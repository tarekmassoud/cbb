## Ingredients

### Base (1st layer)
- 2 cups medjool dates  
- 1 cup raw almonds  
- 1 cup raw walnuts  
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Coconut (2nd layer)
- 2 cups unsweetened shredded coconut  
- 1/4 cup coconut cream  
- 1/4 cup maple syrup or honey  
- 2 tbsp coconut oil  
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Chocolate (3rd layer)
- 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips  
- 2 tbsp coconut oil

## Instructions
1. Blend base ingredients to a sticky crumble; press into a lined 20×20 cm pan.  
2. Mix coconut layer; spread evenly over the base.  
3. Melt chocolate with coconut oil; pour over the top and smooth.  
4. Refrigerate 3–4 hours until firm; slice into squares.
