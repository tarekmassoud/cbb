## Ingredients
- 2 cans tuna, drained  
- 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped  
- 1/4 cup spring onion, finely chopped  
- 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped  
- 2 tbsp mayo  
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard  
- Juice of 1 lemon  
- Salt & pepper, to taste  
- Lettuce, tomato, capers  
- Sourdough or favorite bread, toasted

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine tuna, celery, spring onion, parsley, mayo, Dijon, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix until creamy.  
2. Toast bread. Add lettuce and tomato, then spread tuna mixture and scatter a few capers on top. Serve.
