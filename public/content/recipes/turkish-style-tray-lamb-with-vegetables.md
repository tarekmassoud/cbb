## Ingredients

- 1.200 g lamb meat, cubed
- 1/2 kg small onions
- 1/2 kg cherry tomatoes
- 3 thin-skinned green sweet peppers
- 1 green hot pepper
- 8 to 10 garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup olive oil

## Ingredients

### Spices

- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp paprika
- 3 bay leaves

### Sauce

- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp sumac

## Instructions

1. Throw everything together with the peppers and mix well.
2. Cover and bake for 1 hour at 180°C.
3. Then uncover, add the sauce, cover again, and bake for another 1 hour until the meat becomes tender and flavorful.
4. Enjoy with homemade bread and a cup of ayran yogurt drink.
