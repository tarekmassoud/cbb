## Ingredients
- 1 package fusilli pasta  
- 200 g cherry tomatoes  
- 100 g salami, sliced  
- 2 roasted bell peppers, sliced  
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced  
- 1 bunch parsley, chopped  
- 20 mini mozzarella balls  
- Black olives, pitted

### Vinaigrette
- 1/2 cup red wine vinegar  
- 1/2 cup olive oil  
- 3 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 tbsp honey  
- 1 tsp oregano  
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Boil pasta, drain, and rinse cold.  
2. Toss with vegetables, mozzarella, and olives.  
3. Whisk dressing ingredients and pour over.  
4. Mix well and chill before serving.
