## Ingredients
- 125g unsalted butter, softened  
- ¾ cup (160g) brown sugar  
- ⅓ cup (75g) caster sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 large egg  
- 1¾ cups (225g) all-purpose flour  
- ¼ tsp baking soda  
- 100g dark chocolate chips  
- 100g strawberry chocolate chips  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with parchment.  
2. In a bowl, cream butter with brown sugar, caster sugar, and vanilla until smooth.  
3. Beat in the egg. Mix in flour and baking soda until a dough forms. Fold in chocolate.  
4. Roll ½-cupfuls (about 80 ml) of dough into balls and place on tray. Refrigerate 30 minutes.  
5. Bake 15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely on tray before enjoying.
