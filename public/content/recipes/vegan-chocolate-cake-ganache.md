## Ingredients
Dry:
- 2 cups plain or cake flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup cocoa powder
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt

Wet:
- 1 1/4 cups almond or coconut milk
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 tsp instant coffee or espresso powder
- 1/4 cup coconut oil or melted butter substitute
- 1 tsp vanilla

Ganache:
- 2 cups dark chocolate
- 2/3 cup coconut cream

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease a 23 cm springform tin.
2. Whisk dry ingredients. Whisk wet ingredients. Combine until smooth.
3. Pour into tin, tap to release bubbles, and bake 25–30 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean. Cool.
4. Melt chocolate with coconut cream over low heat. Cool slightly, then whisk until thick and spread over cake.
