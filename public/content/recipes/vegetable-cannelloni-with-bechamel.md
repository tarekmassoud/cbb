## Ingredients

### Veggie Filling
- 1 bunch spinach or chard, blanched and chopped  
- 2 leeks, sliced  
- 4 cloves garlic, minced  
- 6 carrots, diced small  
- 5 zucchini, diced small  
- 250 g mushrooms, sliced (reserve a few for topping)  
- 1 medium onion, diced  
- 1–2 tbsp sunflower oil, plus more as needed  
- Salt and pepper

### Béchamel & Pasta
- 3 tbsp flour  
- 1 L milk  
- 120 g mozzarella or similar cheese, grated  
- 250 g dried cannelloni tubes

## Instructions
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil; sauté leek, onion, and garlic 5 minutes.  
2. Add carrots 5 minutes; add zucchini and most mushrooms; cook 15 minutes. Season; stir in chopped greens; set aside.  
3. For béchamel, whisk 3 tbsp oil with flour 2 minutes; slowly add milk and cook to thicken. Season.  
4. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a 25×30 cm dish.  
5. Pipe veggie mix into cannelloni; arrange in dish. Cover with sauce and mozzarella; dot with reserved mushrooms.  
6. Bake at 200ºC for 30–35 minutes until golden and bubbling.
