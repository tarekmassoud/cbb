## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive or coconut oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 celery stalk, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp garam masala, plus a pinch for garnish
- 1 tbsp tomato purée
- 250 g green lentils, rinsed
- 3 dry bay leaves or 1 green leaf
- 700 ml vegetable stock
- Small bunch fresh coriander, chopped
- 2 sweet potatoes (about 400 g)
- 4 floury potatoes (about 600 g)
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender; drain. Mash together and season; set aside.
2. Heat oil over medium heat. Sauté onion, carrots, and celery with a pinch of salt for 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic, chili flakes, cumin, and garam masala; cook 2 minutes.
4. Stir in tomato purée, lentils, bay leaves, and stock. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook 15–20 minutes until lentils are tender.
5. Stir in chopped coriander and adjust seasoning.
6. Spoon the lentil stew into a baking dish. Top with the mash, roughing the surface.
7. Bake at 200°C for 25–30 minutes until golden and bubbling. Garnish with a pinch of garam masala.
