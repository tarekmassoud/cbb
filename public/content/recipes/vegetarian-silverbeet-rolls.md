## Ingredients
### Leaves
- 2 bunches fresh silverbeet  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1 tsp lemon juice  

### Filling
- 1 large onion, chopped  
- 1 tsp black pepper  
- 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas  
- 1 cup long-grain rice, washed  
- 3 medium tomatoes, finely chopped  
- 1 cup parsley, chopped  
- 1 tbsp chopped mint  
- 3 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 small can tomato sauce  
- 2 tsp sumac  
- 1/2 cup olive oil  

## Instructions
1. Mix filling ingredients.  
2. Blanch silverbeet leaves until softened.  
3. Fill and roll tightly.  
4. Place rolls in pot with tomato slices at bottom.  
5. Add remaining filling liquid + water.  
6. Cook 40 min at 180°C, then 20–25 min at 170°C.
