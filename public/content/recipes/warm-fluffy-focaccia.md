## Ingredients
- 500 g white bread flour
- 420 g warm water
- 12 g dry yeast
- 1 tsp honey
- 1 tbsp olive oil, plus more for pan and topping
- 2 tsp sea salt

## Instructions
1. Mix warm water, yeast, honey, and oil. Stir in flour and salt to form a sticky dough.
2. Rest 10 minutes, then stretch and fold. Rest another 10 minutes; repeat.
3. Grease a bowl, place dough seam-side down, oil the top, cover, and refrigerate 24–48 hours.
4. Turn onto a greased tray (22×33 cm). Oil hands and gently stretch to the edges; rest 2 hours.
5. Preheat oven to 220°C. Dimple the dough with oiled fingers and drizzle with olive oil.
6. Bake 20–25 minutes until golden. Cool briefly, slice, and serve.
