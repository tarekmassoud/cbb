## Ingredients

- 14 heads of artichokes
- 300 g chunk meat
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 2 extra lemons (for lemon water)
- 1 teaspoon flour
- Water

## Instructions

1. Prepare a bowl of water with the juice of 2 lemons.
2. Remove the hard leaves of the artichokes, cut each head into 4 quarters, and clean the inside well. Place the cleaned artichokes immediately in the lemon water to prevent discoloration.
3. In a pot, heat some olive oil and sauté the chopped onion until translucent.
4. Add the meat and cook for a few minutes on the fire. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add water, cover, and let the meat cook until tender.
6. Add the artichoke quarters to the meat. Add more water if needed and let everything boil and cook together until the artichokes are tender.
7. In a separate pan, heat a little olive oil and add 1 teaspoon of flour. Stir on the fire until lightly browned.
8. Add a little of the cooking water from the artichokes and whisk well until you get a smooth sauce.
9. Pour this mixture back into the pot and let it cook for a few more minutes.
10. Finally, add the juice of 2 lemons, mix gently, and serve hot with steamed rice.
