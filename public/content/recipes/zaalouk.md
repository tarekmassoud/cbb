## Ingredients

- Olive oil
- 4 tomatoes, diced
- 2 grilled eggplants (ready-made, from Plein Soleil), chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Cilantro, chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 fresh chili, finely diced
- Pomegranate seeds, for garnish
- Fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Heat a generous drizzle of olive oil in a pan.
2. Add the cilantro and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook over medium heat until they soften and start to break down.
4. Add the chopped grilled eggplants along with the salt, turmeric, ginger, cumin, and diced fresh chili. Mix well.
5. Cover and let simmer on low heat for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the texture to become rich and soft.
6. Uncover and mash slightly to reach a dip-like consistency if needed, then stir in the chopped parsley.
7. Finish with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice, a drizzle of olive oil, and a sprinkle of pomegranate seeds.
8. Serve warm or at room temperature with toasted baguette.
