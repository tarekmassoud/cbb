## Ingredients
### Filling
- 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil  
- 1 medium fennel, thinly sliced  
- Sea salt and cracked black pepper  
- 2 tbsp vinegar  
- 1 tbsp light brown sugar  
- 1 cup (240g) ricotta  
- 1 medium yellow and green zucchini, thinly sliced  
- 1 tsp finely grated lemon rind

### Pastry
- 100g unsalted butter, chopped  
- ⅓ cup water  
- 1½ cups (210g) flour  
- ½ tsp sea salt flakes  
- 1 tsp fennel seeds, crushed

## Instructions
1. For pastry, pulse ingredients, adding water to form a dough. Chill 30 minutes.  
2. Roll to a 30 cm round and place on a lined tray. Preheat oven to 200°C.  
3. In a pan, sauté fennel in oil with salt and pepper. Add vinegar and sugar; cook until caramelized.  
4. Spread ricotta over pastry leaving a 5 cm border. Top with fennel and zucchini; sprinkle lemon rind.  
5. Fold edges over and press. Bake 40 minutes or until golden. Optional: drizzle with honey and serve.
